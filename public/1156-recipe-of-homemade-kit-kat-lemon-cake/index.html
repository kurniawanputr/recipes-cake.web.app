The ingredients needed to make Kit-Kat Lemon Cake:
  1. Get Duncan Hynes Lemon Cake Mix
  2. Make ready Eggs
  3. Prepare water
  4. Make ready vegetable oil (add little extra for a moister cake)
  5. Get ground cinnamon
  6. Make ready a drizzle of honey
  7. Prepare Betty crocker cream cheese frosting
  8. Prepare crushed up kit-kat bar
  9. Prepare round cake pans
  10. Take non-stick spray and/or crisco
Steps to make Kit-Kat Lemon Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. In a large mixng bowl put cake mix, water, eggs and oil and mix until all lumps are gone and mix is completely dissolved.
  2. Spray or coat pan with crisco
  3. Evenly pour mix into both cake pans
  4. Drizzle a little honey over both cakes and add a dash of cinnamon (however much u prefer)
  5. Put cake in oven for about 25-28 minutes until center is firm and fluffy
  6. Let cake cool in pan at least an hour
  7. Take out first cake and put on to a plate. Cover the first half with frosting.
  8. Then take kitkat bar and crush it. Pour a little on top of cake
  9. Then put other cake on top and cover top half with frosting. And add the rest of candy ontop of cake.
  10. Enjoy (:
