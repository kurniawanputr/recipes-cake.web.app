Instructions to make Simple vanilla cake recipe (charcoal jiko baked):
  1. Gather all ingredients and tools
  2. In a clean bowl put the flour, pinch of salt and baking powder and mix together
  3. In another bowl put the eggs and whisk untilll fluffy then add sugar and margarine and mix them together…and milk to the mixture until you achieve consistency
  4. Add the dry mix to the wet mix and continue whisking until its smooth
  5. Prepare your sufuria by greasing with margarine and dust with a little flour
  6. Add your paste into the sufuria
  7. Lit your jiko and put another big sufuria with salt or sand inside to heat for 10 minutes
  8. Put your small sufuria in the big sufuria and cover it with a tight lid and add some charcoal on top
  9. Bake for 50 minutes and remove it from the jiko to cool
  10. Once cooled you can put the cake on a plate and enjoy with a cold beverage or your choice
