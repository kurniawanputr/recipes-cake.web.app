The ingredients needed to make Tea Pound Cake. Simple Tea Time Cake:
  1. Get 110 grams Flour
  2. Make ready 90 grams Sugar
  3. Get 30 ml Oil
  4. Take 30 ml Milk
  5. Take 3 Eggs
  6. Get 2 tsp Baking Powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  8. Take 1/4 tsp Orange Food Color
Steps to make Tea Pound Cake. Simple Tea Time Cake:
  1. 1st separate the Egg yolk and Egg White.
  2. Then in Egg yolk and Vanilla Essence.mix it..take egg white in Bowl and Blend it with sugar.add egg yolk and white together and mix it.
  3. Now strain the dry ingredients in this and mix it.
  4. Pour the batter in cake Mould and place it in Preheated pan for 45-50 minutes.
  5. Your cake will be ready in 50 minutes.
  6. You can serve it with Tea.
