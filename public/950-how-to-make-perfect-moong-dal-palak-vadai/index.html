The ingredients needed to make Moong dal palak vadai:
  1. Take leftover moong dal (cooked)
  2. Get spinach leaves (leftover) from soup
  3. Make ready small cup gram flour
  4. Prepare small cup wheat flour
  5. Prepare tbspoons rice flour
  6. Prepare tbspoons ginger garlic and chilli paste
  7. Prepare tspoon turmeric powder
  8. Get tspoon sesame seeds
  9. Make ready tspoon salt
  10. Make ready tbspoons oil
  11. Make ready chopped coriander leaves with stems

Steps to make Moong dal palak vadai:
  1. Take moong dal and spinach in big vessel.
  2. Add to it chopped coariander leaves,ginger garlic and chilli paste,sesame seeds 1 tspoon,salt and all flours.
  3. Mix well and make soft dough.we don't require water as moong dal is cooked.and spinach leaves are blanched.that much water is sufficient to form a dough.make little cylindrical shape pieces of dough.
  4. Prepare steamer grease steaming plate with oil.put it on a steamer and steam for 15 minutes. allow it to cool.
  5. Cut into pieces. Heat Pan add oil to it.once hot add sesame seeds add cut pieces and cook till golden brown or crisp. Flame should be low.or it will burn.
