The ingredients needed to make Taro Cake / Savory / Vegan recipe:
  1. Make ready frozen taro, thawed and diced
  2. Get tapioca flour
  3. Take rice flour
  4. Take garlic mince
  5. Get lt water
  6. Make ready preserved radish, rinsed
  7. Prepare sweet soy sauce
  8. Prepare shallot chopped
Steps to make Taro Cake / Savory / Vegan recipe:
  1. Fry chopped garlic and add diced taro, stir well, add water and flour, simmer for 15 mins till soft, set aside
  2. Place into stainless steel container and steam for 15mins
  3. For the topping, fry shallot and radish together, add sweet soy sauce and pepper
  4. Serve the taro cake with topping
