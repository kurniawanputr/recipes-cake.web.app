The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready 1 box yellow cake mix
  2. Take 1 cup peanut butter
  3. Take 1 cup light brown sugar
  4. Take 1 1/4 cup water
  5. Get 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  6. Make ready 3 eggs
  7. Get 1/2 tsp powdered sugar (optional)
Instructions to make Peanut Butter Pound Cake:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. combine all ingredients in bowl, mix until smooth
  3. pour batter in bundt pan
  4. bake for 55 mins. do not over cook so it stays chewy.
  5. sprinkle with powdered sugar, enjoy!
