Instructions to make Vietnamese Lemongrass Pork Bowl:
  1. In a pan, add oil and onions, lemongrass, green chillies.
  2. Let it cook for about 3 mins, add garlic in, move everything to the side of the pan.
  3. Add pork in the middle of the pan and let it cook for about 5 mins.
  4. Mix the pork with the onions and etc.
  5. Add 1 tbsp brown sugar and 1 tbsp oyster sauce.
  6. Let it cook until the liquid has evaporated.
  7. Mix 1 tsp corn flour and 5 tbsp of water well and add them into the pan.
  8. Sprinkle white pepper / black pepper on top and mix well.
  9. Finally, garnish with red chillies and coriander and egg.
  10. Serve with Rice.
