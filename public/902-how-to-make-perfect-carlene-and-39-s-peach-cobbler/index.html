The ingredients needed to make Carlene's Peach Cobbler:
  1. Make ready Butter
  2. Get Flour
  3. Take Sugar
  4. Take Baking Powder
  5. Prepare Baking Soda
  6. Make ready Salt
  7. Prepare Milk
  8. Prepare Peach Pie Filling (21 oz)
  9. Prepare Blueberries
Instructions to make Carlene's Peach Cobbler:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350
  2. Melt butter in 9"x13" pan (don't burn)
  3. Mix dry ingredients w/ fork
  4. Add milk
  5. Stir and spread into pan w/ melted butter
  6. Add peach pie filling and blue berries
  7. Let sit for 5 minutes
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes
  9. ENJOY!
