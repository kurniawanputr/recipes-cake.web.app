The ingredients needed to make Kumquat Pound Cake:
  1. Prepare 150 grams 〇Unsalted butter (cultured)
  2. Get 20 grams 〇Heavy cream
  3. Make ready 140 grams ◎Sugar
  4. Take 20 grams ◎Honey
  5. Get 10 grams ◎Kumquat syrup
  6. Prepare 3 Egg (large size)
  7. Get 165 grams Cake flour
  8. Make ready 5 grams Baking powder
  9. Get 150 grams Candied kumquats
  10. Take 100 ml Kumquat syrup

Steps to make Kumquat Pound Cake:
  1. Bring all the ingredients to room temperature. Drain the syrup from the candied kumquats.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients and sift. Lightly grease the pound cake pans with shortening or butter, then line with kitchen parchment paper.
  3. Cream the butter well, add the heavy cream, and beat until fluffy. Add the sugar, honey, and syrup one at a time, and beat well after each addition.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well with each addition. Fold in the dry ingredients. Mix in the candied kumquats. It's good if you let it rest overnight in the refrigerator.
  5. Pour the batter into the cake pans and smooth the surfaces with a spatula. Bake in an oven preheated to 180°C for 35 minutes (please adjust according to your oven).
  6. Once baked, immediately coat with the kumquat syrup. Let sit for half a day, wrap with plastic wrap, and chill in the refrigerator for 1~2 days.
  7. The moist and fragrant pound cake is ready!
