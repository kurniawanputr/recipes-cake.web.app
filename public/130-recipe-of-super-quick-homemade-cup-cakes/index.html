The ingredients needed to make Cup cakes:
  1. Get 1/2 cup maida
  2. Get 1 cup coco powder
  3. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  4. Take 1/3 chocolate
  5. Take 4 _5 drops vanilla essence
  6. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Take 4 tsp Oil
  9. Prepare 6-8 tbsp Milk

Steps to make Cup cakes:
  1. In a bowl combine all above ingredients very well.
  2. Heat oven 180°c
  3. Now pour batter into a baking pan.
  4. Place into oven for 15 mins.
  5. Once it done let it cool.
