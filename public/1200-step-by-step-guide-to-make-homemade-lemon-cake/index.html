Instructions to make Lemon cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 175°C
  2. Grease and flour (8 by 1/1/4) baking pan
  3. Mix the butter and 2 cups granulated sugar in a bowl until light and fluffy
  4. Add eggs 1 at a time and lemon zest
  5. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a bowl.
  6. In other bowl combine 1/4 cup lemon juice, butter milk and vanilla.
  7. Add flour and butter milk mixture alternately to the batter, beginning and ending with the flour.
  8. Divide the batter evenly and bake for 45 min to 1 hour until a cake tester comes out clean.
  9. When cake is done allow it to cool for 10 minutes. Remove cake from the pan and set on a tray to cool completely.
