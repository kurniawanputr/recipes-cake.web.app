The ingredients needed to make banana pound cake:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup criso or lard
  2. Prepare 2 stick butter
  3. Take 2 cup sugar
  4. Take 1 cup brown sugar
  5. Make ready 3 cup self rising flour
  6. Take 1 cup milk
  7. Take 5 eggs
  8. Take 2 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Take 3 ripe bananas
  10. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions to make banana pound cake:
  1. preheat the oven to 350
  2. soften the butter
  3. cream the two sticks of butter,lard, brown sugar ,and sugar together for at least 3 minutes until butter,lard,brown sugar,and sugar is smoothed
  4. then add the 5 eggs one at a time
  5. after adding each egg one at a time add the two teaspoons of vanilla extract and the teaspoon of baking powder
  6. then add the 3 cups of self rising flour one cup at a time also scrape the sides of the bowl to get the excess flour off the sides
  7. finally adding the cup of milk pouring a little bit at a time and still mixing until the batter is smoothed
  8. smash the three bananas in to the batter mix slowly
  9. spray and Flour a bunt pan or a pound cake pan. pour the batter in the pan making sure there is equal amount on both sides. place the pan in oven for 45 mins to 1 hour and 25 mins depending on your stove
