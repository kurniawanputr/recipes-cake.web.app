The ingredients needed to make Vegan Chocolate Cake:
  1. Make ready 180 g All purpose flour
  2. Make ready 60 g cocoa
  3. Take 120 g Sugar
  4. Get 1 tsp Baking Soda
  5. Take 1/4 tsp Salt
  6. Prepare 1/3 Cup Vegetable Oil
  7. Get 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  8. Make ready 1 Cup Hot water
  9. Take 1 Tbsp White Vinegar
  10. Get Sugar Syrup
  11. Prepare 1/3 Cup Hot Water
  12. Get 1 Tbsp Cocoa
  13. Prepare 2 Tbsp Sugar
  14. Take 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  15. Make ready Icing
  16. Prepare 1/3 Cup Powdered Sugar
  17. Get 2 Tbsp Cocoa
  18. Prepare 1/2 Cup Caramel
Instructions to make Vegan Chocolate Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees, prepare a baking tin with butter, flour and lining the bottom with baking paper
  2. Sift the flour, 1/2 cup cocoa, 1 cup sugar, baking soda and salt together.
  3. Add the wet ingredients, vegetable oil, 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract, 1 cup Hot water and white vinegar to the dry ingredients, mix well and pour into the prepared baking dish. Bake between 20-25 min until a skewer comes out clean.
  4. In the meantime while the cake is baking prepare the syrup by mixing the hot water, cocoa, sugar and vanilla in a separate jug.
  5. When the cake is cooked, take it out the oven, pearcing it all over with holes. Pour the syrup all over the cake, the syrup will quickly be absorb. Leave to cool completely.
  6. Mix the powdered sugar, cocoa and caramel and spread over the cooled cake. Optional: add chopped hazelnuts
