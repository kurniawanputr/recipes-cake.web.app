The ingredients needed to make Lemon Mint Honey Cake:
  1. Take Wet Ingredients–
  2. Make ready Egg
  3. Get Milk
  4. Take Sunflower oil
  5. Get Sugar
  6. Make ready Vanilla essence
  7. Get Dry Ingredients–
  8. Get Maida / All-purpose flour
  9. Get Baking powder
  10. Take Baking soda
  11. Get Salt
  12. Get Others–
  13. Prepare Mint leaves
  14. Take part Lemon juice
  15. Make ready Water
  16. Take Honey
  17. Take Mixed fruit jam
  18. Get Coconut shavings
Instructions to make Lemon Mint Honey Cake:
  1. Beat all wet ingredients well.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in wet ingredient mix. Mix it gently in to batter.
  3. Grind mint leaves and lemon juice and extract the juice.
  4. Divide batter into two halves. Add mint juice in one half and mix it.
  5. Grease a pan with oil and place a butter pepper. Pour both batter one after other.
  6. Make some decoration using a toothpick.
  7. Heat a tawa and place batter over tawa. Bake for 15-20 minutes by closing the pan.
  8. Check the cake after 15 minutes using toothpick. If it's non sticky, remove the pan from tawa and let it cool for 10 minutes.
  9. Make a liquid with honey and 5 TSP of water in a pan. Let it cool and pour it over cake.
  10. Heat jam and and 1 TSP water in a pan and let it cool. Then apply it over cake as topping.
  11. Add some coconut shavings and the cake is ready to be served.
