The ingredients needed to make Banana Pound Cake made with Rice Flour:
  1. Make ready A
  2. Take 2 medium Banana
  3. Take 80 grams Sugar
  4. Prepare 60 grams Canola oil
  5. Prepare 70 ml Water
  6. Make ready B
  7. Make ready 130 grams Rice flour
  8. Prepare 30 grams Cornstarch
  9. Get 1/2 tbsp Baking powder

Steps to make Banana Pound Cake made with Rice Flour:
  1. Mash the banana with a fork. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Add the A ingredients to a bowl in order from the top. Use a whisk to combine.
  3. Add the B ingredients into the bowl. Mix together well and then pour in the cake pan. Bake for 30-40 minutes.
