The ingredients needed to make Glazed pound cake:
  1. Make ready for the cake
  2. Make ready 3 cup ap flour
  3. Get 3 cup sugar
  4. Get 1 lb butter
  5. Prepare 1 cup milk
  6. Get 1/2 lb shortening
  7. Make ready 5 eggs
  8. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  10. Take glaze
  11. Take 2 cup powdered sugar
  12. Get 1/4 cup butter
  13. Make ready 3 tbsp milk or cream
  14. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla extract or almond extract

Instructions to make Glazed pound cake:
  1. Cream together butter and shortening add sugar slowly a little at a time until light and fluffy
  2. Add eggs (room temp) one by one making sure each egg is well incorporated before adding the next
  3. Add 1/3 of your flour mix well followed by 1/2 of your milk continue until theres nothing left (make sure u start with flour and end with flour).
  4. Stir in vanilla extract and preheat oven to 350° (grease and flour cake pan) pour batter in and cook for 1hour to 1:30 mins depending on oven until toothpick inserted comes out clean
  5. For the glaze melt butter in a microwave or the stove top add to the powder sugar and add extract plus milk and whisk together add more or less cream for which ever consistency u prefer enjoy :-)
