Instructions to make Caramel apple crisp:
  1. Skin and slice apples
  2. Coat apples in cinnamon, flour, white sugar. Then spread the apples in a casserole dish or baking pan.
  3. Add caramel sauce or Make caramel sauce. To make it, combine 1 can sweetened condensed milk, 2 cups of brown sugar, 1 tablespoon light corn syrup, and 3 tablespoons of caramel syrup (optional) over low heat on the stove. Mix until itâ€™s a smooth mixture. Let cool. Spread over the apples.

  5. Sprinkle the oat mix on the caramel apples.
