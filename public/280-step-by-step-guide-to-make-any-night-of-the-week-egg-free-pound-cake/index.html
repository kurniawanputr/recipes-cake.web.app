The ingredients needed to make Egg-free Pound Cake:
  1. Get 200 grams Pancake mix
  2. Prepare 200 ml Milk
  3. Take 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
  4. Get 2 tbsp Sugar
Instructions to make Egg-free Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Put all the ingredients into a bowl and mix well.
  3. Grease the pound cake tin with oil (not listed) and pour in the mixture from Step 2.
  4. Bake at 180℃ for 15-20 minutes. Poke a skewer through it to check if it's done.
