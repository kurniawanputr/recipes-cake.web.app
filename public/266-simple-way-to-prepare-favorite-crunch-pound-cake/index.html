The ingredients needed to make Crunch Pound Cake:

  2. Prepare 2 cup cake flour
  3. Get 1 cup crisco
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1 tsp lemon extract
  6. Make ready 6 eggs
  7. Get 1 tbsp baking powder

Steps to make Crunch Pound Cake:
  1. Cream crisco and sugar together.
  2. Add in one egg at a time, mixing well between each egg, then add flavoring.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix flour and baking powder.
  4. Sift dry ingredients into cream mixture.
  5. Do not preheat oven. Bake cake on 300° for one hour.
