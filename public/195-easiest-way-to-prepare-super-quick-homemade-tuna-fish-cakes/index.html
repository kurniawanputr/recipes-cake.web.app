The ingredients needed to make Tuna Fish Cakes:
  1. Get 150 g Shredded Tuna
  2. Make ready 3 Medium potatoes, cooked and mashed
  3. Take Salt and ground black pepper, for taste
  4. Take 1 Egg
  5. Make ready 1/2 Onion
  6. Get 1 Clove garlic
  7. Prepare 1 tsp butter
  8. Prepare Plain flour
  9. Get Bread crumbs
  10. Prepare Oil for frying
  11. Make ready 1 Tbs chopped fresh parsley
Steps to make Tuna Fish Cakes:
  1. Fry chopped onion and garlic with butter.
  2. Drain the canned tuna and put it aside.
  3. In a bowl add mashed potatoes, egg, salt and pepper; then mix
  4. Add the fried onion and garlic, fresh chopped parsley and tuna to the mashed potato mix.
  5. Sprinkle flour on a clean table, put your mixture on it and flatten it with your hands. Cut circles with a cup.
  6. Coat first in egg and then bread crumbs, do this to all the cakes. (Once done you can let them cool a bit in the fridge for about 15 minutes while you clean up.)
  7. Shallow fry your fish cakes each side for about 4 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Serve with salad 🥗
