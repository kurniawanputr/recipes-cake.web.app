Instructions to make Soy milk lemon cake:
  1. Sift flour and baking powder.
  2. Whisk sugar, essence, margarine and eggs then add the flour mixture alternating with soy milk.
  3. Mix together to form a consistent runny butter. Add lemon juice and some raisins (optional).
  4. Pour the mixture in a greased baking tin and bake in a preheated oven for 35min.
  5. Enjoy.
