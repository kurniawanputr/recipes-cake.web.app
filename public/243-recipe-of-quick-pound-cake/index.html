The ingredients needed to make Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready 225 g sugar (2.5 dl)
  2. Take 225 g butter
  3. Make ready 4 large eggs
  4. Get 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  5. Get 225 g flour (3.75 dl)
  6. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 1,5 tbsp baking powder
  8. Make ready 180 ml sour cream/yogurt

Steps to make Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degree celcius.
  2. Mix together butter and sugar until it pale, approximately 8 minutes using electric mixer.
  3. Add egg one by one.
  4. Add Vanilla extract.
  5. On the other bowl, prepare the dry ingredients. Mix together flour, salt and baking powder.
  6. By continuing mix the batter, pour half of the flour and sour cream/yogurt.
  7. Pour the rest of the flour and mix until it well incorporated.
  8. Pour the batter into the cake tin and bake for 75-80 minutes.
