The ingredients needed to make Pound cake:
  1. Make ready 2 cup Maida
  2. Prepare 1 tsp Baking powder
  3. Prepare 1 pinch Baking soda
  4. Take 1 pinch Salt
  5. Make ready 1 1/4 cup Butter
  6. Get 1 1/2 cup Powder sugar
  7. Get 4 eggs
  8. Take 1 tsp Vanilla essence

Steps to make Pound cake:
  1. Add butter on room temperature and beat it
  2. Add powder sugar in three steps and beat it
  3. Add eggs 1 by 1 and beat it
  4. Add vanilla essence beat it leave the mixture
  5. Now here dry ingredients in maida add baking powder and soda and salt mix it very well
  6. Add dry ingredients in wet ingredients and mix it the batter is ready now
  7. Grease the baking pan and add butter paper and grease it
  8. Preheat the oven on 180 degree for 30 mins
  9. When your oven is preheated put the batter in it and bake it for 45 minutes on 180 degree
  10. When the cake is baked take it out and enjoy it with tea
