The ingredients needed to make Choco chips Cup cakes:
  1. Prepare 1.25 cups all-purpose flour(maida)
  2. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  3. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1/4 cup oil
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Get 1/4 cup chocochips
  7. Get 3/4 cup yogurt
  8. Prepare Pinch salt
  9. Get 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup water

Instructions to make Choco chips Cup cakes:
  1. In a bowl, add yogurt,oil, vanilla essence and whisk it
  2. Seive maida, powered sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt.
  3. Whisk well. Add water.
  4. Add chocochips. Mix gently.
  5. Add 1 scoop cake batter and put some chocochips on it.
  6. Preheat oven for 10 minutes at 180 degree.
  7. Bake it for 15 minutes at 180 degree.
