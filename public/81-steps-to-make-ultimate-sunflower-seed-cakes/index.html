The ingredients needed to make Sunflower Seed Cakes:
  1. Take 1 cups sunflower seeds
  2. Take 5 tablespoon fine cornmeal
  3. Take 1-1/2 cups water
  4. Take 2-1/2 teaspoon maple syrup
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup oil to fry with
Steps to make Sunflower Seed Cakes:
  1. Heat the water in a pot add the sunflowers and boil then simmer for 1 hour.
  2. Drain the water and mash the sunflower seeds into a paste. All of them doesn't have to be smashed, Heat the oil.
  3. Mix the sunflower paste with the maple syrup and cornmeal till it forms a thick dough.
  4. Form into small cake and fry them in the oil.
  5. Turn after as needed.
  6. Move to paper towels to absorb excessive amounts of oil.
  7. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
