Instructions to make Chocolate Cakes with Coconut:
  1. In a bowl,mix first wet ingredients. Stir it well. Add dry ingredients except baking powder.
  2. Add baking powder and stir. Put in rectangular glossy pan then put inside the oven,open down 20 minutes then open up till the cake finish.
  3. In a small pot mix the chocolate mixture then put in steamer,stir continuously until become hot.
  4. Cut the cake into small square, using fork take the cake and deep it to the chocolate and then put in coconut powder. Repeat this until finish.
  5. Put in any tray and design as you want. Enjoy.
