The ingredients needed to make Crab Cakes:
  1. Get 16 oz canned crabmeat, drained
  2. Make ready 1/2 c old-fashioned oats
  3. Make ready 1/2 c egg whites
  4. Prepare 1/4 c chopped fresh cilantro
  5. Prepare 1-1/2 T lime juice
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp each salt and black pepper
  8. Take 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
Instructions to make Crab Cakes:
  1. In large bowl, combine all ingredients until thoroughly mixed.
  2. Firmly form into 6 patties, about 1/2" thick.
  3. Bring large skillet sprayed with cooking spray to medium heat. In batches, cook patties until golden brown and cooked through, about 4 minutes on each side.
