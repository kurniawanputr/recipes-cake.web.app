The ingredients needed to make My Chili Cheese Hominy Bake:
  1. Prepare (15.5) oz cans Golden Hominy
  2. Take ground beef
  3. Make ready (14.5) oz can fire roasted tomatoes
  4. Make ready taco seasoning
  5. Get ground cumin
  6. Get onion powder
  7. Take garlic powder
  8. Take dried cilantro
  9. Make ready Salt
  10. Take sharp cheddar cheese
  11. Prepare pepper jack cheese
  12. Make ready mozzarella cheese
  13. Make ready green onions diced
Instructions to make My Chili Cheese Hominy Bake:
  1. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In a medium skillet cook ground beef if you need to drain it, drain it then add seasonings. If you don't need to then add seasonings + salt.
  3. Add tomatoes and more seasonings if needed.
  4. In a baking dish add the hominy, ground beef mixture, cheeses, green onions and sprinkle garlic powder and more cilantro on the top.
  5. Pop in the oven for 30-45 minutes. With 5 minutes remaining turn broiler on to get the cheese bubbly and brown.
