The ingredients needed to make Lemon cake:
  1. Get 1 tablespoon baking powder
  2. Get 1 cup lemon juice
  3. Get 3 cups all purpose flour
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon bakingsoda
  5. Take 1 cup sugar
  6. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanila essence
  8. Take I tablespoon lemon zest
  9. Take 2 eggs
  10. Take 2 cups milk
  11. Prepare 1 cup Melted butter

Steps to make Lemon cake:
  1. Pre heat oven at 150° for 25 min
  2. In a bowl add in your dry ingredients and and give a good mix.
  3. In a separate container mix in all the wet ingredients and mix it.
  4. Gradually add in the mixture in the dry ingredients while you give a good whisk and bake in your pre heated oven for 30 min.
