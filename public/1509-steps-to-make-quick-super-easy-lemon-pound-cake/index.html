The ingredients needed to make Super easy lemon pound cake:
  1. Get Duncan hines lemon supreme cake mix
  2. Make ready (3.4 oz) instant lemon pudding mix
  3. Make ready eggs
  4. Get water
  5. Take vegetable oil

Instructions to make Super easy lemon pound cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour 10-inch Bundt® or tube pan.
  2. Combine cake mix, pudding, oil, eggs, and water in a large bowl
  3. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes. Pour into prepared pan
  4. Bake for 50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
  5. cool cake on cooling rack for 25 minutes. Then remove cake from pan to cool completely
  6. Add your favorite glazed icing and enjoy
