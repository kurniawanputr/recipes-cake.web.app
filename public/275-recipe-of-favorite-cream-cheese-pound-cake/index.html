The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Pound Cake:
  1. Prepare 1 stick Butter
  2. Make ready 1 cup Shortening
  3. Take 8 oz Cream cheese
  4. Make ready 3 1/2 cup Sugar
  5. Take 6 Eggs
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Vanilla
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Almond extract
  8. Take 1 cup Milk
  9. Make ready 3 cup Cake flour, you can use AP flour but it will change the texture
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Baking powder
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Salt
Steps to make Cream Cheese Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 300Â°F.
  2. In a stand mixer cream together butter, shortening, cream cheese and sugar til light and fluffy.
  3. In a separate bowl combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each egg. Add vanilla and almond extract.
  5. Add flour mixture alternately with milk to creamed mixture til well combined. Pour into well greased and floured pans. I use a loaf pan AND a bundt pan. Recipe makes a lot of batter. U could use a 9x13 or layer pans.
  6. Bake for an hour and 15 minutes to an hour and a half, or til top springs back and cake is golden brown.
