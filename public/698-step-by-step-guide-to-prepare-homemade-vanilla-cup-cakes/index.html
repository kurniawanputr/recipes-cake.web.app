Instructions to make Vanilla cup cakes:
  1. In a bowl put your sugar and butter, cream till fluffy and light
  2. Break your eggs and beat them in a different bowl
  3. Add your beating eggs bit by bit to the cream butter and mix thoroughly
  4. Add flour bit by bit mixing it, then add flavour and baking powder
  5. Grease your pan with butter and bake for 40 to 45mins, enjoy with your hot tea or juice
