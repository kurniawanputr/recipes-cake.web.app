Instructions to make Lemon cake#my healthybakingrecipe#:
  1. Mix the butter with sugar till well mixed.
  2. Add 5 ups of flour and 3 tsp baking powder.
  3. Add the eggs, milk and vanilla essence.
  4. Mix properly using a hand mixer till fluffy.
  5. Grate some lemon cover for effective aroma.
  6. Pour the mixture on a greased tray.
  7. Bake on a preheated oven at 190degrees for 30-40minutes.
  8. Serve
