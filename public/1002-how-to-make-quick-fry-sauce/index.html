The ingredients needed to make Fry Sauce:
  1. Make ready Ketchup
  2. Get Sriracha
  3. Take Lime Juice
  4. Take Sandwich Spread
Instructions to make Fry Sauce:
  1. Add desired amount of ketchup.
  2. Add a squirt Sriracha to give it a hint of spicy. Mix and adjust to your personal taste.
  3. Squeeze a little lime juice approximately half a limes worth.
  4. Finally we're going to add the sandwich spread and this is only to make the sauce a little more creamy.
