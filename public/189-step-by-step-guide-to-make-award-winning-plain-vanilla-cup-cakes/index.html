The ingredients needed to make Plain vanilla cup cakes:
  1. Get 250 g butter(1 simas)
  2. Prepare 2 cups flour
  3. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 6 eggs
  5. Get 1 tbspn baking powder
  6. Take 1 tbspn milk flavour
  7. Make ready 1/2 tspn vanilla flavour
