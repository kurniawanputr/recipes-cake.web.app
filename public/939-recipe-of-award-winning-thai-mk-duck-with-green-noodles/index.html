The ingredients needed to make Thai MK Duck With Green Noodles:
  1. Take For The Duck
  2. Get Large Duck Breasts - trimmed of Silver Skin and Excess Skin
  3. Get Chinese 5 Spice
  4. Prepare Dark Brown Sugar
  5. Prepare Ginger Powder
  6. Prepare red food colouring in 3 Tbl Spoon Water (for red Skin - Optional)
  7. Make ready For The Sauce
  8. Make ready White Peppercorns
  9. Take coconut Milk
  10. Take Duck stock (I use the liquid from the Sous Vide Bag - removed the fat)
  11. Take Garlic
  12. Get Soy Sauce
  13. Get Dark Soy
  14. Prepare Five Spice
  15. Take Ginger Powder
  16. Get Honey
  17. Get Cilantro or coriander
  18. Make ready Thai Fermented Soybean Paste (can get at most large or Asian Stores)
  19. Prepare White Sesame Seeds Crushed (optional)
  20. Get Green Noddle's or Rice to serve
Steps to make Thai MK Duck With Green Noodles:
  1. Mix the Ginger, Sugar and Five Spice and rub on the Meat side of the Duck, Try to avoid any touching the skin
  2. Turn the duck over and brush the red food colouring on the skin and allow to air dry for about 5 mins
  3. Brown the Skin in a hot pan to render the Fat about 4-5 mins. Then I Vacuum seal and Sous Vide at 76.7 for 8 hours OR Pre Heat oven 210c then Roast the duck Skin side up in oven for 15-20 mins depending how well done you like the duck.
  4. For the Sauce Pound the Peppercorns or blitz in a spice grinder. Then add Garlic and Cilantro and blitz into a paste.
  5. Add The Soy Bean Paste, Ginger and Five Spice. and mix together.
  6. Heat a small Pan add a little oil and quickly fry off the spice mix for about 1-2 mins until aromatic then add the duck stock, coconut milk, Soy and honey. Simmer for 5 mins mixing all together.
  7. To Serve I use Green Noodles with the Duck on Top and then spoon over some of the amazing sauce.
