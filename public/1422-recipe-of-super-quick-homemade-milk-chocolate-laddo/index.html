Instructions to make Milk chocolate laddo😇😇:

  2. In a pan boil water add sugar and boil 5 minutes.when it start thickened just immediately add milk powder mixture
  3. Keep stirring at very low flame and when mixture becomes totally thick add ghee
  4. Add powdered nuts in the end.let it cool.grease your palm with ghee and make small laddoos.
