The ingredients needed to make Long Island Iced Tea:
  1. Take light rum
  2. Prepare gin
  3. Take silver tequila
  4. Prepare vodka
  5. Take triple sec
  6. Make ready fresh lemon juice
  7. Take simple syrup
  8. Get Coke
  9. Take Lemon wedge for garnish
Steps to make Long Island Iced Tea:
  1. Mix all ingredients except Coke in a cocktail shaker with ice
  2. Pour into a glass with ice. Garnish with lemon wedge. Add splash of Coke to give the appearance of tea.
