Instructions to make Lemon cake:
  1. Pre-heat your oven to 175degrees C
  2. Oil and dust your baking tin and set aside.
  3. Sift your flour and set aside
  4. In a bowl cream your sugar and margarin
  5. Beat one egg at a time while adding to the cream. Add the milk and water
  6. Add the lemon juice, zest, vanilla essence and fold in the flour to a batter for about 3 mins. Ensure there are no moulds.
  7. Pour into your baking tin.
  8. Bake for 40mins. Insert a toothpick to check if ready.
  9. Set aside to cool.
  10. Serve with your favourite drink.
