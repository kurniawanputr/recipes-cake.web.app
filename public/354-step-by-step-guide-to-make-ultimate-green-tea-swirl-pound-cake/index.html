The ingredients needed to make Green Tea Swirl Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready 100 grams Unsalted butter
  2. Get 90 grams Granulated sugar
  3. Take 2 Eggs
  4. Prepare 100 grams Plain cake flour
  5. Get 3 grams Baking powder
  6. Get 2 drops Vanilla essence
  7. Get 30 grams ◎Milk
  8. Prepare 10 grams ◎Matcha
Instructions to make Green Tea Swirl Pound Cake:
  1. Leave the butter and egg out at room temperature. Mix the ◎ ingredients well. Sift together the flour and baking powder.
  2. Line a loaf tin with baking paper. Preheat the oven to 355F/180C.
  3. Combine the eggs and sugar in a bowl and whisk well. Stir in the vanilla essence.
  4. Put softened butter in another bowl and whisk until it has a mayonnaise-like consistency. Add the sifted flour.
  5. You will have a crumbly texture first, but continue to whisk.
  6. Whisk until creamy. Add the mixture from Step 3 to the bowl in 3-4 batches. Continue to whisk throughout.
  7. Whisk until you have a creamy batter with soft consistency. Divide the batter into two bowls. Add the ◎ into one of the bowls and mix well.
  8. You will have 2 types of batter.
  9. Put the two types of batter alternatively in the prepared loaf tin. Quickly stir with a spoon.
  10. Bake in the oven heated to 355F/180C for about 40 minutes. After 15 minutes of baking, take out the tin and make a slit in the middle lengthwise.
  11. By making a slit in the middle, you will have a pretty-looking cake like this.
