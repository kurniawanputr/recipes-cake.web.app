The ingredients needed to make Sour Cream Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready 1 cup butter (room temperature)
  2. Prepare 2 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Take 3 cup cake flour, (plain all-purpose works fine) measure after sifted
  4. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla
  5. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 6 eggs, separated
  7. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 8 oz sour cream (1/2 pint)

Steps to make Sour Cream Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 300°F
  2. Grease and flour a tube pan
  3. Cream butter until light and fluffy
  4. Add sugar gradually and continue to cream until light and fluffy
  5. Add vanilla and salt
  6. Blend in egg yolks 1 at a time blending well after each addition
  7. Add soda to sour cream and stir
  8. Add flour and sour cream alternately, beginning and ending with flour
  9. Fold in beaten egg whites
  10. Pour into greased and floured tube pan
  11. Bake at 300°F for 1 1/2 hours
