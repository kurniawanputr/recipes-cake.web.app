The ingredients needed to make Taco Pizza:
  1. Make ready Pizza dough
  2. Take ground beef
  3. Make ready taco seasoning
  4. Get refried beans
  5. Make ready salsa
  6. Get shredded Cheddar cheese
  7. Make ready Sliced olives
  8. Take Lettuce, tomatoes, onion and sour cream

Instructions to make Taco Pizza:
  1. Let pizza dough rest 20 minutes
  2. Brown meat in skillet, add taco seasoning and water and simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 400
  4. Spread out pizza dough and bake for 8 minutes
  5. Mix refried beans and salsa. Spread a thin layer on crust
  6. Top with meat cheese and olives.
  7. Bake for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted
  8. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, onions and sour cream
