The ingredients needed to make Muruku:
  1. Prepare idli rice
  2. Take roasted gram (pottukadalai)
  3. Get red chillies
  4. Get curry leaves
  5. Take garlic pods
  6. Make ready butter
  7. Make ready sesame seeds black
  8. Prepare ajwain seeds
  9. Make ready Asafoetida
  10. Prepare Salt as required
  11. Make ready Oil to deep fry

MuRuku is a deep-fried snack item made in Tamil homes. MuRuku, the Tamil verb-root means to "to twist" something. The noun, MuRuku, the name of the snack, is derived from this verb.

Instructions to make Muruku:
  1. Soak idli rice for atleast 5 hours. Drain water and grind it in a wet grinder with very little water.
  2. While it’s grinding, grind red chilly, curry leaves and garlic with water in a blender/mixie. Add this paste also to the wet grinder to grind along with rice. The batter should be thick as well as soft. We can add water later if required.
  3. Powder the roasted gram using mixie/blender and sieve it. Add this to the batter along with salt, ajwain seeds, sesame seeds, asafoetida and butter. Mix well. The dough should be non sticky. If it’s not spicy as expected we can add red chilly powder.
  4. Heat a pan with oil on high heat.
  5. Fill muruku press with the dough. Grease backside of stainless steel lid or laddle with oil and press the dough in round shape. Drop it in oil. Flip and fry both the sides until it becomes crispy. Maintain high heat throughout the process.
  6. It’s ready 😋
