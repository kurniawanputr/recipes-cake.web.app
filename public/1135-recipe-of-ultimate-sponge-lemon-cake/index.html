Instructions to make Sponge lemon cake:
  1. Blend your butter until it completely soft. Add the eggs one at a time.
  2. Add the dry ingredients: Flour, baking powder and sugar. Blend well. Here you can add your milk slowly to a desired consistent.
  3. Add the lemon zest.. If you desire more of the lemon taste a little bit of lemon juice is fine to add(optional). More milk to make it abit smooth.
  4. Preheat your oven as your prepare your baking bowl.. 25 to 30 minutes baking time at 150 °c.
  5. Ps: If you add more of the ingredients for a larger cake. Please ensure you consider the baking time too.
  6. Enjoy with your warm cup of milk. Or your tea.
