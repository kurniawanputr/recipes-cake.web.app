Steps to make Sponge cake with gas ✨ Cake recipe without oven:
  1. Mix egg and sugar until it makes form.
  2. It should look like the picture
  3. Sieve the flour and gently add to the eggs.
  4. Use spatula to mix flour and egg mixture well. Add oil and mix it gently. (Do not over mix, it makes the sponge cake don't come out nicely)
  5. Spread the butter or margarine in the pot you want to use. Pour the dough.
  6. Put the lid on the pot and bake with gas with very low heat for 20 minutes. After 20 minutes, use toothpick or skewer to check the dough. If skewer doesn't get wet with the dough, it's done.
  7. Stop the heat and wait, until it cools down. Sponge cake is done now!
  8. When it's cool, then slice the dough into half.
  9. In a different bowl add 1tbsp sugar, 1/4 tbsp rum and form the heavy cream with mixser.
  10. Cut the fruit as you like and put fruits between sponge cakes, cover with cream and decorate with fruits. Done!
