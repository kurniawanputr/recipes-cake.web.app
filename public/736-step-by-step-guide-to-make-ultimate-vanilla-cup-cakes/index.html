Steps to make Vanilla cup cakes:
  1. Sieve the dry ingredients together and keep aside.
  2. Mix all wet ingredients.
  3. Mix dry ingredients mixture to wet ingredients and mix it well.
  4. Fill the cup cake liner half and bake at 180 for 20-25 min.
  5. While cupcakes are baked wip the wipping cream for icing.
  6. Take a tooth pic and dip it into red gell colour and than mix mix into wipped cream.
  7. Now check the cup cakes with a toothpick after 20 mins and if come out clean, it's done.
  8. Cool it on wirerack.
  9. Fill the whipped cream in a piping bag with a nozzel of your choice.
  10. When cupcakes cool completely done your icing with whipped cream.
  11. Now cup cakes are ready to serve.
