Steps to make Lemon cake:
  1. Beat oil and sugar together. Add eggs one by one and Beat till fluffy. Add lemon juice,peels and food colour
  2. Fold in your flour alternating with milk to a smooth constitency.
  3. Pour your butter in an oiled baking tin and bake for 45-50 min at 150 degrees
  4. When ready let it cool in a wire rack.
