The ingredients needed to make Corn Casserole:
  1. Prepare whole kernel corn, drained (15.25oz)
  2. Make ready cream style corn (14 3/4 oz)
  3. Take package corn muffin mix (recommended: Jiffy)
  4. Take butter, melted
  5. Get sour cream
  6. Make ready Shredded cheddar cheese
Steps to make Corn Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the 2 cans of corn, corn muffin mix, sour cream and melted butter.
  3. Pour into a greased 9 by 13 inch casserole dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven and top with cheddar. Return to oven for 5 to 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  5. Let it stand for at least 5 minutes and then serve warm.
