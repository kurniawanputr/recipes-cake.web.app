The ingredients needed to make Funnel Cakes:
  1. Take eggs 1 cups of milk 1 cup water
  2. Make ready vanilla extract 3 cups all-purpose flour
  3. Prepare sugar 3 teaspoons baking powder 1/4 teaspoon salt
  4. Make ready Oil for deep frying Confectioners' sugar to sprinkle on top
Steps to make Funnel Cakes:
  1. Mix all ingredents together in a large mixing bowl
  2. Pour batter into a zip lock gallon bag
  3. Cut corner of bag, bigger the cut faster and thicker the batter will come out
  4. Pour into pre heated oil in a large pot/deep dish pan. Make circles of all different sizes as u pour batter in.
  5. Give about 2-3 mins a side or until side is golden brown.
  6. Remove cooked funnel cake place on baking sheet with a papertowel underneath to soak up some of the oil
  7. Sprinkle Confectioners sugar on top.
  8. And Enjoy!!!
