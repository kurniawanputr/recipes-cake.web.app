Instructions to make Lemon cake:
  1. In a bowl, mix sugar & margarine together
  2. Add the eggs as you continue mixing
  3. Then the flour. Sifted if you prefer
  4. Add in vanilla essence & lemon flavor & mix in just a little bit.
  5. After greasing your tin with a little margarine & flour, pour in your cake mixture and bake
  6. As your cake bakes, prepare lemon juice by mixing icing sugar & lemon juice (freshly squeezed). Make it runny enough.
  7. With your cake out of the oven, prick holes on top of it using toothpick & drizzle your lemon juice over the top.
