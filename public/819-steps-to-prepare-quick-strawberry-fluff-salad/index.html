The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Fluff Salad:
  1. Get Cottage Cheese
  2. Prepare Heavy Whipping Cream
  3. Prepare Sweeter (we mix our own)
  4. Take Sugar Free Strawberry Jello
  5. Make ready Frozen Strawberries sliced
Steps to make Strawberry Fluff Salad:
  1. Put 2 cups of heavy cream and 1 tsp sweetner in large bowl and whisk untill stiff peaks form.
  2. Add in all other ingredients and mix thoroughly. Refrigerate overnight.
