Instructions to make Banana cake recipe:
  1. In a bowl mix margarine and sugar and beat till creamy
  2. Add eggs one by one and also add vanilla essence while mixing
  3. Add the bananas after mashing them and mix gently
  4. Sieve flour and baking powder on your mixture
  5. Put your butter in the oven
  6. Ice your cake and decorate as you wish
