The ingredients needed to make Chicken Curry (Filipino-Style):
  1. Prepare chicken
  2. Get ginger
  3. Make ready whole garlic
  4. Make ready red onion
  5. Make ready potatoes (cubed)
  6. Take carrots (cubed)
  7. Make ready red bell peppers
  8. Prepare whole gata (coconut milk)
  9. Prepare Curry Mix Powder (McCormick)
  10. Prepare green sili

This Filipino recipe is a creamy stew with a mild curry flavor, cooked with coconut milk, potatoes and bell peppers.

Instructions to make Chicken Curry (Filipino-Style):
  1. Pan fry thawed-out chicken for 1-2 mins each side
  2. Sauté the garlic, onion, and ginger over low heat.
  3. Add chicken in pan.
  4. Add 1 red bell pepper, potatoes, and carrots.
  5. Add gata (coconut milk)
  6. Add betsin and pepper and 1/3 of Curry Mix Powder (McCormick)
  7. Let simmer until carrots and potatoes are cooked.
  8. Add the rest of Curry Mix.
  9. Add salt, pepper, and betsin to taste. Add a pinch of sugar.
  10. Add bell pepper and green sili.
  11. Let simmer for 10 mins.
