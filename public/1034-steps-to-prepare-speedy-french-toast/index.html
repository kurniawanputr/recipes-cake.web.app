Instructions to make French toast:
  1. Crack the eggs and mix well with milk.
  2. Pour mixture on top of bread and let it sit for a few minutes until bread has absorbed the mix.
  3. Place some butter on a pan and turn the heat on.
  4. Cook the bread until it is solid. You can either add brown sugar or maple syrup. Enjoy!!
