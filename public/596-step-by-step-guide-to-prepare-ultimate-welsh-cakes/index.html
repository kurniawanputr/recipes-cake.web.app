The ingredients needed to make Welsh Cakes:
  1. Prepare self-raising flour
  2. Make ready salt
  3. Make ready equal parts cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger
  4. Get butter
  5. Take caster sugar
  6. Take egg
  7. Get milk
  8. Prepare blueberries
  9. Take dark chocolate chips
Steps to make Welsh Cakes:
  1. Mix sifted flour, spices and salt together in a large bowl
  2. Using your fingertips rub the butter into the dried ingredients
  3. Sift in the caster sugar and mix well
  4. Beat together the egg and milk then mix in with the other ingredients until you have a stiff dough
  5. Divide the dough into two bowls adding chocolate chips to one bowl and blueberries to the other (mixing well)
  6. Roll each of the doughs between baking paper until about 0.5cm thickness and then refrigerate for 1 hour
  7. Using a cookie cutter cut out circles of the dough
  8. Preheat a frying pan with a little butter, then carefully place in the welsh cakes
  9. Cook the welsh cakes for 4 minutes then flip and cook for another 4 minutes until golden brown and risen slightly
  10. Remove from the heat, sprinkle with a little caster sugar and serve
