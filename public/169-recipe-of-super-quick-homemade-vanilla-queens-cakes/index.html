The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Queens cakes:
  1. Get 7 cups self raising flour
  2. Get 2 cups milk
  3. Make ready 2 cups sugar
  4. Get 6 eggs
  5. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Make ready 500 g melted margarine

Instructions to make Vanilla Queens cakes:
  1. Sift your flour and mix all the dry ingredients in the bowl
  2. On the other bowl beat your eggs, milk and vanilla essence
  3. On the dry ingredients mix your melted margarine stir and add the milk bowls and stir
  4. Then pour on your cup cake holder
  5. Set your oven into 180 degrees and it must be baked for 30 minutes
  6. Your queens cakes can be served with any drinks
