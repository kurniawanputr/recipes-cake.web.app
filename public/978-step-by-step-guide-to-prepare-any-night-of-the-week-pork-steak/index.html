Instructions to make Pork Steak:
  1. Prepare the marinade. Mix calamansi juice, soy sauce, black pepper, and crushed garlic together in a bowl.
  2. Let the meat marinate for a minimum of 3 hours.
  3. Then, pan fry the pork chops for 1-2 mins. each side on medium heat.
  4. Remove porkchops. Sauté garlic (from the marinade) and chopped onions.
  5. Add the pan-fried porkchops to the pan with the marinade.
  6. Add 1 beef cube and 2 cups of water. Let it simmer on medium heat with lid on for 45-60 mins, until porkchop is tender.
  7. Add butter.
  8. Add sugar and pepper to taste.
  9. Add onion ring garnish. Simmer until onions are cooked.
