The ingredients needed to make Cup cakes frosting:
  1. Prepare 125 g butter
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Get 11/2 cup flour
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla flavor
  5. Prepare 1 TBS baking powder
  6. Prepare 3 eggs
  7. Get Sprinklers
  8. Make ready Colour pink
  9. Make ready Purple
  10. Prepare Green
  11. Prepare Whipped cream
  12. Take 1 cup whipped cream
  13. Get 1 cup icing sugar
  14. Prepare 1 tsp powder milk
