The ingredients needed to make Outer Space Cake:
  1. Make ready Popcakes
  2. Get 120 gr flour
  3. Get 80 gr sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Get Pinch salt
  6. Take 3 eggs
  7. Take 100 gr butter
  8. Prepare Food colors
  9. Get Dark Chocolate Cake
  10. Get 80 gr flour
  11. Take 60 gr sugar
  12. Prepare 15 gr dark cocoa powder
  13. Get 3 eggs
  14. Get 30 ml milk
  15. Prepare 30 gr butter
Steps to make Outer Space Cake:
  1. Popcakes: Beat eggs and sugar in a bowl and well mix in a water bath until it becomes fluffy. Add butter and eventually the flour (gradually). Divide into some parts and add food colors. Pour and randomly mix your batter into a popcake mold. Bake for about 15-20 mins at 170 degcel.
  2. Dark Chocolate Cake: Beat eggs and sugar in a bowl and well mix the black cocoa sponge in a water bath until it becomes thick. Add flour gradually then followed by warm milk and butter. I bake this half and half. Half plain and half combine with popcakes inside it.
  3. Decorate it as you wish and enjoy!
