The ingredients needed to make Pound cake.:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup flour
  2. Make ready 3 large eggs
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp milk
  5. Take 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder or soda
  8. Take 3/4 cup butter. use a bit over 3/4 cup

Instructions to make Pound cake.:
  1. mix eggs, milk and vanilla (make sure all is room temp)
  2. mix flour, sugar, salt, baking powder.
  3. add butter to flour mix and half egg mix. mix well.
  4. add rest egg mix and mix well for 3 mins
  5. grease loaf pan, bake 350 for 40-50 mins. enjoy!
