Instructions to make Cup cakes:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Line the baking tray with the cupcake parchment paper
  3. Sieve the flour together with baking powder and salt
  4. Whisk the margarine and sugar and add eggs one by one
  5. Add vanilla essence
  6. Add flour in batches alternating with milk and fold in until it's well done
  7. Bake for 20 minutes or until ready at 180 degrees
