Instructions to make New year cake recipe:
  1. Cream butter and sugar using a hand mixer or whisker
  2. Add in eggs one at a time
  3. Add milk
  4. Stir in vanilla flavor
  5. Sift over the dry ingredients, flour,baking powder and baking soda
  6. Add raisins
  7. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan
  8. Sprinkles some mini chocolate chips on top and bake in a preheated oven @350°F for 35min
  9. Allow it to cool
  10. Then slice and enjoy
