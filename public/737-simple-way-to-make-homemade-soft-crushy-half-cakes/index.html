Steps to make Soft crushy half cakes:
  1. In a bowl add the flour,sugar, cardamon and baking powder whisk well and keep aside.
  2. Heat the oil then pour it in the flour mixture mix until well combined then add water bit by bit while kneading until the dough is well combined and soft.
  3. Heat the oil in a medium heat.Deep fry the half cakes until cooked and golden brown on both sides.
  4. Drain excess oil then let it cool.Ready to serve
