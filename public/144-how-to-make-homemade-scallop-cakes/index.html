The ingredients needed to make Scallop cakes:
  1. Take 1 cup chopped (processor) scallops
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon fresh parsley
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon fresh cilantro
  4. Get 1 teaspoon chives
  5. Take 1 egg
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  7. Take 2 teaspoons flour
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  9. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  10. Take 1/4 cup chopped celery
  11. Get 1 teaspoon chopped shallot
  12. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon lime juice
  13. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  14. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon olive oil
  15. Get Bread crumbs until lightly thick

Instructions to make Scallop cakes:
  1. Mix all ingredients lightly in a mixing bowl
  2. Pan fry with olive oil until golden brown on both sides
  3. Place cakes on a baking sheet at 350 for 5-7 minutes
