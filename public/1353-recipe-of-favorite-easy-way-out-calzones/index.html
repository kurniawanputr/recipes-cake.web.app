The ingredients needed to make Easy Way Out Calzones:
  1. Get store bought pizza dough, either pre made or the mix
  2. Prepare pizza sauce
  3. Prepare shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. Get Your favorite pizza toppings (I used salami, pepperoni and black olives)
  5. Make ready salted butter
  6. Make ready pepper
  7. Make ready garlic powder
  8. Prepare oregano
  9. Take Shaved parmesan cheese
Instructions to make Easy Way Out Calzones:
  1. Make the dough per package instructions or take the ready made dough and form four balls. Roll each ball out until thin and round.
  2. Add 1/4 cup sauce to each, spread it evenly and then add the toppings and cheese.
  3. Fold them up into pockets shaped like half moons. Make sure they are sealed up well so they don’t burst while baking. You can use a fork to press the edges down and don’t be afraid to pinch them either.
  4. Soften the butter in a microwave safe bowl, 30 seconds at a time. Add the pepper, garlic salt and oregano, and mix well.
  5. Place the finished calzones on a foil lined, greased sheet pan, seam sides down. Brush the butter generously on top of each one.
  6. Throw a little parmesan on top and bake at 400 degrees for about 20 minutes.
  7. Let them rest for a few minutes then enjoy!
