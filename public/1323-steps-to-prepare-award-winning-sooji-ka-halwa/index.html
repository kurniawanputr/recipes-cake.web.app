Instructions to make Sooji ka Halwa:
  1. Roast sooji in Desi Ghee till a sweet smell comes from it.
  2. Put sugar in it and keep stirring
  3. Put boiled water in it and keep stirring till the ghee comes out
  4. Then put all the dry fruits and your sooji halwa is ready🙂
