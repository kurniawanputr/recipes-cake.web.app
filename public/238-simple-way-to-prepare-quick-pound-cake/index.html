The ingredients needed to make Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready 2 sticks butter
  2. Get 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  3. Take 3 cups sugar
  4. Get 5 eggs
  5. Prepare 3 cups cake flour
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon fine salt
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  8. Make ready 1 cup milk
  9. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  10. Make ready 1 can Baker’s Joy

Steps to make Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cream butter and vegetable oil together
  3. Add one egg at a time, beating after each addition
  4. Stir dry ingredients in a bowl and add to mixer alternately with milk, starting and ending with flour
  5. Mix in vanilla
  6. Spray Baker’s Joy to cover all sides of the pan and pour mixture into bundt pan
  7. Bake for 1 the 1 1/2 hours, until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean
  8. Let the cake sit for 10 minutes and remove cake from pan
