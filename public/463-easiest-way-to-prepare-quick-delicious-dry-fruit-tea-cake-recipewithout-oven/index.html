The ingredients needed to make Delicious Dry Fruit  Tea Cake Recipe(Without Oven):
  1. Prepare Eggs (3)
  2. Get Oil (1 cup)and 2 Tbsp Oil for grease the Cake Mold
  3. Get Icing Sugar (1 cup)
  4. Prepare Maida (1 and half cup) Shifted
  5. Make ready Vanilla Essence (1 Tsp)
  6. Get Baking Powder (1 Tsp)
  7. Make ready Some Roasted Walnut
Steps to make Delicious Dry Fruit  Tea Cake Recipe(Without Oven):
  1. Take a blender and add Egg and Blend it When they Fluffy add Icing Sugar Oil, Vanilla Essence And Blend it When batter is Fluffy Then Put All the batter in Large Bowl then add Maida Or baking powder and Properly Mix it
  2. Take a Cake Mold Grease with oil and Put Butter Paper in cake mold.and again Grease with oil and Dust some Maida
  3. Then add All the cake batter And Sprinkle some Peanut. - And Then bake it for Half Hour.
