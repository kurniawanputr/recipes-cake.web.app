Instructions to make Lemon cake:
  1. Sieve the dry ingredients flour,salt and baking powder
  2. Cream the sugar and butter until fluffy
  3. And egg bit by bit alternating with flour
  4. Add lemon zest and lemon juice and mix. Avoid over mixing
  5. Preheat oven and bake in an oven for 45 mins at 180 degrees.
