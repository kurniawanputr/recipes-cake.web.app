Instructions to make Dinner Rolls:
  1. In the bowl of a stand mixer combine the warm milk, melted butter, sugar, and yeast. Let stand 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients to the mixer bowl and mix until combined and pulling off the walls of the bowl.
  3. To test if it’s done, tear off a small piece of dough and stretch into a thin layer. If the dough doesn’t rip, it’s ready. Or, press a finger into the dough to see if it bounces back.
  4. Lightly grease a large glass bowl with oil. Add the dough, cover with a damp towel, and let rest until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough and knead, just until the dough is smooth, about 2 minutes.
  6. Divide the dough into 12 equal parts.
  7. Knead the balls, gathering the edges of the dough toward the center to make a taut, round ball with a smooth top.
  8. Place the rolls on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise for 30 minutes.
  9. Preheat the oven to 375˚F (190˚C).
  10. Brush rolls with melted butter. Lightly sprinkle the rolls with sea salt.
  11. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the rolls have browned. Brush with melted butter again.
