The ingredients needed to make Easy pumpkin muffins:
  1. Get can plain pumpkin
  2. Make ready box spice cake mix
  3. Make ready chocolate chips and or chopped walnuts
  4. Make ready pure vanilla extract optional

Instructions to make Easy pumpkin muffins:
  1. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Spray muffin tins with baking spray with flour or line with paper muffin cups
  3. Mix everything together with a mixer till well combined
  4. Use an ice cream scooper to scoop out into muffin tins. I made 24 mini muffins plus 2 regular sized muffins.
  5. Bake regular sized muffin for 18 to 22 minutes and mini muffin about 10 to 15 minutes till a tooth pick comes out clean watch them carefully
  6. If you have white or yellow cake mix and want spice cake add 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger, allspice and cloves
  7. Variation ideas: chocolate cake, cinnamon cake, butter pecan cake, gingerbread cake, white or yellow cake,carrot cake, funfetti cake, banana cake
  8. White chocolate chips, dark chocolate chips, mint chocolate chips butterscotch chips, peanut butter chips, cinnamon chips, chopped almonds, chopped pecans, shredded coconut, raisins, shredded carrots or zucchini, mini marshmallows
