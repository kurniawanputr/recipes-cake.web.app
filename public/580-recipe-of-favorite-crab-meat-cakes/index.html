The ingredients needed to make Crab meat cakes:
  1. Take 1 pound crab meat
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup cream sauce
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Make ready 8 ounces mushrooms
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  6. Prepare 4 tablespoons flour
  7. Make ready 2 cups cream
  8. Take to taste Salt,pepper paprika
  9. Get 1/2 cup cooking sherry
