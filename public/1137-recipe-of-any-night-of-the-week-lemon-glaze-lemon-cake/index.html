The ingredients needed to make Lemon Glaze Lemon Cake:
  1. Take lemon zest
  2. Make ready lemon juice
  3. Get all-purpose flour
  4. Make ready powdered sugar
  5. Prepare olive oil
  6. Get big eggs
  7. Get salt
  8. Get baking powder
  9. Take baking soda
  10. Make ready for glaze
  11. Get icing sugar
  12. Get lemon juice
Steps to make Lemon Glaze Lemon Cake:
  1. Mix the eggs and the powdered sugar in a bowl and whisk well
  2. Seive and add the dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, baking Powder, salt) to the bowl and mix well by cut and fold method
  3. Add the lemon zest and the lemon juice to the batter and mix well
  4. Add the batter to the greased pan and bake at 180°C for 35-40 minutes (keep an eye on it, it might take more or less time (
  5. For the glaze, mix the icing sugar and lemon juice in a bowl and let it rest for 3/4 minutes
  6. On the cake, spread the glaze and tadaaa…. Your lemon Glaze is ready. Decorate it your way
