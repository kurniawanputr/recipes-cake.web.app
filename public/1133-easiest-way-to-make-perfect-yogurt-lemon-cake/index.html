Instructions to make Yogurt Lemon Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to gas Mark 5/190°C/ 375°F. In large bowl, mix yogurt, eggs, sugar, and oil. Mix well.
  2. Add lemon zest and lemon juice, mix again. Add flour and salt, mix gently until incorporate.
  3. Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for 40-50 minutes
  4. Put it in cooling rack for 10 minutes. Serve and enjoy it.
