Steps to make Lemon sponge cake:
  1. Make sponge by mixing eggs and sugar, then add flour and whisk gently. Bake at 200'C for 30 minutes, rest for a night before cutting
  2. Make lemon curd with zest of 2 lemons, 117 g of lemon juice, egg yolks, butter and sugar heated in one pan till they get the thick substance. Cool down before using.
  3. Make Swiss meringue with 4 yolks, lemon juice and sugar.
