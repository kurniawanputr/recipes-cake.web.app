The ingredients needed to make Potato cakes:
  1. Take 3 middle sized red skin potatoes
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp flour
  3. Take to taste salt and pepper
  4. Prepare Oil for frying
  5. Take Any type of dip you like

Instructions to make Potato cakes:
  1. Clean and peel potatoes
  2. Grate them
  3. In the dish mix potatoes, flour, salt and pepper
  4. Put 1 tbsp of the mix on a heated pan with oil and fry 3 min on each side until gold in color
  5. After frying, place them on a paper towel for a minute to get rid of oil
  6. Serve with burgers, meat or any kind of dip
