The ingredients needed to make Lemon Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready pound cake
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cup cake flour, plus 2 tablespoons
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup, salted butter, room temperature
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  8. Make ready 3 large eggs
  9. Take 2 tsp pure vanilla
  10. Get 2 tsp lemon zest, equals about two large lemons
  11. Take 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
Steps to make Lemon Pound Cake:
  1. Cream butter and sugar in large mixing bowl on medium high for 4 minutes. You want to incorporate as much air into this.
  2. Beat in eggs, one at a time.
  3. Mix in ricotta,lemon juice, vanilla and zest, scraping sides after each addition.
  4. Add in your dry ingredients and mix until just incorporated.
  5. Pour batter into greased loaf pan and bake in preheated 350°F oven for 45- 55 minutes.
  6. Loaf is done when toothpick is inserted into center and comes out clean.
  7. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before turning cake out.
  8. Recipe by taylor68too.
  9. For glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons lemon juice. Stir until liquid is mixed well into sugar. Drizzle over cake.
