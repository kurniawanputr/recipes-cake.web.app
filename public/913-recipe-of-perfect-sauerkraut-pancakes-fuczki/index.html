The ingredients needed to make Sauerkraut “pancakes” - fuczki:
  1. Get Sauerkraut
  2. Take chickpea four (I used it for extra protein, but any flour will be as good)
  3. Take oil
  4. Take Smoked paprika, cumin
  5. Prepare soy sauce or a big pinch of salt

Instructions to make Sauerkraut “pancakes” - fuczki:
  1. Mix flour with 1/4 cup of water, soy sauce, paprika and cumin
  2. Add sauerkraut and oil, mix well
  3. Fry on a dry pan (but can also add oil to it for more intensive taste)
