The ingredients needed to make Yeasted Lemon Cake:
  1. Prepare flour (All purpose) keep aside 11/2 cups flour
  2. Get eggs (room temperature)
  3. Make ready salt
  4. Take yeast
  5. Make ready lemon rind
  6. Make ready melted butter (approximately 2/3rd cups)
  7. Take milk (warm)
  8. Take sugar
  9. Make ready Lemon Syrup
  10. Make ready water
  11. Get lemon juice
  12. Make ready sugar
Steps to make Yeasted Lemon Cake:
  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, yeast, salt & sugar)
  2. Combine the warm butter and milk.Then add this to the dry ingredients and whisk vigorously until everything is well mixed.
  3. Add the eggs and whisk well, add 11/2 cups flour and whisk to a smooth batter.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased 10inch tube or ring pan.Cover and let raise for 30 min.
  5. Preheat oven as 10 min before the dough finishes rising.Bake for 30-40 minutes or until a skewer inserted in the middle come out clean.
  6. For the lemon syrup: Place water and sugar in a sauce pan and bring to boil.Let cool and stir in the lemon juice.
  7. Pour the lemon syrup on the cake once out of the oven…let the cake cool…then remove it from the tin and serve!!
