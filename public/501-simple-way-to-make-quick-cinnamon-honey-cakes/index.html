The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Honey Cakes:
  1. Take flour
  2. Make ready baking powder
  3. Take salt
  4. Take very soft butter
  5. Take More butter (for greasing pan)
  6. Prepare more butter (for glazing)
  7. Take lemon worth of zest
  8. Get nutmeg
  9. Make ready milk
  10. Take eggs
  11. Prepare honey (raw is best)
  12. Take vanilla
  13. Take cinnamon
Instructions to make Cinnamon Honey Cakes:
  1. Grease the bejezus out of the muffin pan with soft but not melted butter.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350
  3. Mix all of the dry ingredients in a bowl. Add in soft butter and mix until it's an evenly crumbly mixture
  4. In a small bowl whisk together the milk, eggs, 3/4 cup honey, and vanilla.
  5. Mix the wet and dry ingredients until combined. Don't over mix. It'll still be a little lumpy.
  6. Pour batter into muffin pan. Bake for 16 minutes or until cakes are not quite done.
  7. Microwave 1/8 cup melted butter and remaining 1/4 cup honey. Add 3-4 dashes of salt. Microwave for 10 seconds and stir.
  8. Remove hot cakes from muffin pan to parchment-Iined sheet pan
  9. Use a pastry brush to paint the hot cakes with the honey butter mixture. Be generous and let it run down the sides. Let sit for 6-10 minutes so honey can soak in.
  10. Bake again for 9 minutes.
  11. Let cool and serve with butter or lemon whipped cream.
