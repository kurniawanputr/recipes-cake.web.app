The ingredients needed to make Easy to bake lemon pound cake:
  1. Prepare 2 boxes lemon cake mix
  2. Get 1 box (3.4 oz) lemon instant pudding
  3. Get 6 eggs
  4. Get 2 cups water
  5. Prepare 2/3 cup oil
  6. Take Lemon glaze:
  7. Get 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  8. Get 1-2 Tbsp milk
  9. Take 1/2 tsp lemon flavor

Instructions to make Easy to bake lemon pound cake:
