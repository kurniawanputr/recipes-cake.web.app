The ingredients needed to make Rainbow pound cake:
  1. Prepare 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  4. Get 1 pinch salt
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon rinds
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp orange rinds
  7. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Take 1/2 cup margarine
  10. Make ready 1 cup plain yorgut
  11. Prepare 1 tbs vanilla essence
  12. Prepare different food colors
  13. Get 1/4 cup milk

Steps to make Rainbow pound cake:
  1. Pre-heat oven at 180°C and grease and dust the baking pan
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients, including the sugar, in one bowl. Add in the margarine and rub in to a breadcrumb texture
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and add them int the flour mixture
  4. Add iin the yorguta and mix to a smooth batter
  5. Add in the rinds and whisk till well combined.
  6. Divide the batter int 5 dishes and in each container, add in a 1 tsp of powder food color and 2 tbsp milk. mix till well combined
  7. Pour the batter into the greased baking tin, alternating one after another. tap from the bottom to removr=e excess bubbles. bake in the preheated oven for 40 minutes
  8. Serve and enjoy
