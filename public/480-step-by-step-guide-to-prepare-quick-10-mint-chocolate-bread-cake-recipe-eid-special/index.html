Steps to make 10 mint chocolate bread cake recipe (Eid special):
  1. Take a 5 bread slices cut in the sides. Chocolate syrup preparation. Take a pan add 1 tbsp butter. Add cocoa powder,powder sugar, ND water mix well. Cook for 2-3 mint. Ready. Chill it.
  2. Icing preparation. Take a bowl and whisk chill it 30 mint in a freezer. Half cup whipping cream add in a bowl. Beat it. After 2-3 mint add powder sugar mix it. Add cocoa powder mix it.
  3. Icing ready. Take a bread slice apply chocolate syrup ND apply icing set it. Same procedure in all breads. Decorate icing add 2 tsp cocoa powder mix it. And make a flower
