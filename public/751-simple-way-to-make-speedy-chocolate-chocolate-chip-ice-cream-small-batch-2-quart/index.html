The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chocolate Chip Ice Cream - Small Batch (2 quart):
  1. Take large Egg
  2. Make ready Sugar
  3. Prepare Vanilla
  4. Take square German Sweet Chocolate
  5. Prepare squares Semi Sweet Chocolate
  6. Make ready Heavy Cream
  7. Prepare + quart Milk
Instructions to make Chocolate Chocolate Chip Ice Cream - Small Batch (2 quart):
  1. Slowly mix together Egg, sugar and vanilla.
  2. Melt the chocolates the (approximately 2 minutes in a microwave.) Do not burn!
  3. Add 1/2 of the chocolate to the mix.
  4. Add the cream AFTER the chocolate.
  5. While mixing, drizzle the rest of the chocolate into the mix.
  6. Add milk and our into freezer. (Add more/less milk to fill your freezer.
  7. Freeze per manufacturer instructions.
