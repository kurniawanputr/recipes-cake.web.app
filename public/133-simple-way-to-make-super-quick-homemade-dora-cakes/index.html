The ingredients needed to make Dora Cakes:
  1. Prepare 2 cups Maida
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Baking Soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup Brown Sugar (or normal sugar powder)
  5. Take 3 tbsp Honey
  6. Make ready 1 cup Milk
  7. Make ready 4 tbsp Fresh cream
  8. Make ready 1/3 cup Condensed Milk
  9. Prepare 1/3 cup Refined Oil
  10. Prepare Pinch Salt
  11. Take 1/2 tsp Vanilla essence
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp oil to grease pan
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup Chocolate Sauce or more as per required
Steps to make Dora Cakes:
  1. Take oil in a pan, add sugar and mix well
  2. Add cream and condensed milk and mix.
  3. Add honey and vanilla essence and mix well.
  4. Add milk, once mixed add maida soda, baking powder and salt and mix all the ingredients well.
  5. Now take a non-stick pan, add batter and on low flame cover and cook.
  6. Turn and cook for the other side too.
  7. Once cooled add Chocolate sauce on one back side of the dora cake and cover it with another one to form a sandwich type.
  8. Serve immediately.
