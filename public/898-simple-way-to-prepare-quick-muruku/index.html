The ingredients needed to make Muruku:
  1. Make ready 6 cup idli rice
  2. Take 2 cup roasted gram (pottukadalai)
  3. Prepare 10 red chillies
  4. Get 20 curry leaves
  5. Prepare 6-8 garlic pods
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  7. Get 3 tsp sesame seeds black
  8. Get 3 tsp ajwain seeds
  9. Prepare 2-3 pinch Asafoetida
  10. Get Salt as required
  11. Get Oil to deep fry

Steps to make Muruku:
  1. Soak idli rice for atleast 5 hours. Drain water and grind it in a wet grinder with very little water.
  2. While itâ€™s grinding, grind red chilly, curry leaves and garlic with water in a blender/mixie. Add this paste also to the wet grinder to grind along with rice. The batter should be thick as well as soft. We can add water later if required.
  3. Powder the roasted gram using mixie/blender and sieve it. Add this to the batter along with salt, ajwain seeds, sesame seeds, asafoetida and butter. Mix well. The dough should be non sticky. If itâ€™s not spicy as expected we can add red chilly powder.
  4. Heat a pan with oil on high heat.
  5. Fill muruku press with the dough. Grease backside of stainless steel lid or laddle with oil and press the dough in round shape. Drop it in oil. Flip and fry both the sides until it becomes crispy. Maintain high heat throughout the process.
