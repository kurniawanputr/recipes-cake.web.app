The ingredients needed to make Poppy Seed Lemon Cake:
  1. Prepare Poppyseed
  2. Make ready Flour ( 280 gr)
  3. Make ready Olive oil ( 60 gr)
  4. Take Eggs
  5. Make ready Baking powder ( 40 gr)
  6. Get Salt
  7. Prepare Butter ( 125 gr) at room temperature
  8. Get Fresh Lemon juice
  9. Make ready Heavy cream ( 120 gr)
  10. Get Lemon zest
  11. Make ready Sugar ( 200 gr)
Steps to make Poppy Seed Lemon Cake:
  1. Set your oven on 350°F ( 180 C ) Get two mixing bowls. In one of the bowl mix flour , poppyseed ,baking powder and salt.
  2. In second bowl mix sugar, butter, and heavy cream. Using electric mixer add egg one of the time,add lemon juice, and olive oil.zest.
  3. Transfer this batter into a 30cm x 10cm baking pan , I used loaf pan. Doesn't really matter, (but if your pan is too deep your cake won't be looking good)
  4. Cook about 50 minutes . Check after 40 minutes, to see if is cook, put some dry knife into the cake, if the knife come wet, cook more, if is dry it is cooked. Take it out. While is still hot, put some your favorite jelly, I used fig marmalade .
