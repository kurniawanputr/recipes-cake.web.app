The ingredients needed to make Easy Banana Cake Recipe:
  1. Make ready sugar
  2. Make ready vanilla essence
  3. Get egg
  4. Make ready banana, mashed
  5. Take self-raising flour
  6. Take milk
  7. Prepare butter
Steps to make Easy Banana Cake Recipe:
  1. Preheat oven 170°C;
  2. melt the butter, sugar and vanilla essence in a sauce pan;
  3. remove from heat;
  4. add mashed bananas and stir thoroughly;
  5. add egg and mix well;
  6. stir in flour, add milk and mix thoroughly;
  7. bake at 170°C for approximately 40-50 minutes. (or until a pick inserted comes out clean)
