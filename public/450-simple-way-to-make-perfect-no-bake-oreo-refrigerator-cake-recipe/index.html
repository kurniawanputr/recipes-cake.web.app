The ingredients needed to make No-bake Oreo refrigerator cake recipe:
  1. Take (1 bag) Oreo cookies (crushed) (divided)
  2. Get cup butter (melted)
  3. Get package cream cheese (softened)
  4. Prepare cup sugar
  5. Prepare (1 container) cool whip (frozen topping thawed)
  6. Take package chocolate fudge instant pudding mix
  7. Make ready cup half and half cream (or use whole milk)
Instructions to make No-bake Oreo refrigerator cake recipe:
  1. Crush Oreo cookies,set 1/4 cup aside for topping.
  2. Mix the remaining crushed cookies with the melted butter
  3. Press into the bottom of a greased 13Ã—9 in baking pan.
  4. In a bowl,beat the cream cheese with the sugar on medium speed.
  5. Fold in half of the cool whip topping.
  6. Spread over cookie crumb layer.
  7. Refrigerate 1/2 hour.
  8. In the meantime,combine milk with instant pudding mix,beat well,until smooth.
  9. Pour over chilled mixture in the pan.
  10. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
  11. Spread remainder of cool whip topping on top of pudding layer.s
  12. Sprinkle with remaining reserved cookie crumbs.
  13. Store cake in the refrigerator covered tightly.
