Steps to make Lemon cake:
  1. Cream sugar n blue band till no particles of sugar are visible
  2. Add eggs each at a time,mixing well each egg before you add the other.
  3. Mix the baking powder to the flour and sift
  4. To the egg mixture pout the flour mixture by bits mixing evenly, if too thick add some milk.add in the lemon juice and zest to the batter
  5. Add in the lemon juice and zest mix well.
  6. Grease pan and pour the batter.
  7. Bake for 50mins at 180°
