Steps to make Lemon cake:
  1. Cream the margarine and sugar until light
  2. Add an egg at a time while whisking
  3. Add the lemon and it's juice
  4. Add half of the flour and baking powder mixture and fold gently
  5. Add the milk
  6. Fold in the rest of the flour
  7. Bake
  8. Allow to cool
