The ingredients needed to make Yummy Chocolava cakes:
  1. Take Maida
  2. Get coco powder
  3. Take oil
  4. Make ready sugar
  5. Get salt
  6. Make ready soda
  7. Prepare baking powder
  8. Get vanilla essence
  9. Take Milk
Steps to make Yummy Chocolava cakes:
  1. Take a bowl sieve dry ingredients like 1/2 cup maida, 1/4 cup coco powder, 1/2 cup powdered sugar, 1/4 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp baking powder and pinch salt.
  2. After sieve, all the dry ingredients, now mix it well next add wet ingredients 1tsp vanilla essence, 1/4 cup oil or butter, 1/2 cup milk.
  3. Then whisk and combine all mixture.
  4. Grease Cups or moulds first with oil and then dust with flour.
  5. Bake at 180 for 15 min.Until toothpick comes out clean.
