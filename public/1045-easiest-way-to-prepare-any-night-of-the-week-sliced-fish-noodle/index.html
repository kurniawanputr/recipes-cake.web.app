Instructions to make Sliced fish noodle:
  1. Marinade fish: marinade fish in wine, soysauce and sesame oil for 15min
  2. Boil noodle and put noodle aside on serving bowl
  3. Add ginger and anchovies into boiling water. Add chicken stock. Add vegetables and fish. Add milk to the water. Cook until fish turn opaque. Pour these ingredients into the bowl of noodle. Serve.
