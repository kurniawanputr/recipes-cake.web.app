The ingredients needed to make Creamy Pumpkin 🎃, Sausage and Kale 🥬 Pasta 🍝:
  1. Get Italian sausage (hot OR sweet)
  2. Prepare onion, chopped
  3. Get green pepper, chopped
  4. Get garlic cloves, minced
  5. Make ready black pepper
  6. Prepare nutmeg
  7. Take beef broth
  8. Get 100% pumpkin 🎃 (NOT PUMPKIN PIE FILLING !)
  9. Get cream cheese, softened
  10. Make ready fresh Kale 🥬, rinsed and dried
  11. Take heavy cream
  12. Take Cooked pasta 🍝(16 ounce), any shape
Steps to make Creamy Pumpkin 🎃, Sausage and Kale 🥬 Pasta 🍝:
  1. Remove casings from sausages and sauté in a large stockpot until browned……break into smaller pieces as they cook…..
  2. Add into sausages your chopped onion, green pepper and minced garlic,sauté for several more minutes……
  3. Now add your black pepper, nutmeg, beef broth, 100% pumpkin and cream cheese, heat to boiling, stirring occasionally…..
  4. Next add in your kale, breaking into smaller pieces, heat thoroughly…… Cook until kale has softened…….
  5. Add in your heavy cream, heat thoroughly and stir well……
  6. Add cooked pasta into your sauce, heat, stir well and enjoy 😉!
