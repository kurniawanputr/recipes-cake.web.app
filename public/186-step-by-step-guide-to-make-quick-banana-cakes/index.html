The ingredients needed to make Banana Cakes:
  1. Take 100 grams Lurpak Light
  2. Get 90 grams caster sugar
  3. Get 2 small eggs
  4. Make ready 100 grams self raising flour
  5. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 1 large ripe banana
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Take 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  9. Make ready 3/4 tbsp sesame seeds

Steps to make Banana Cakes:
  1. preheat oven to 170°F celsius
  2. melt the butter in a small saucepan over a medium heat
  3. in a large bowl beat together the eggs, sugar and vanilla extract until the eggs have aerated​ and the sugar is dissolved,
  4. when the butter has melted leave to cool while you weigh out the other ingredients and mash the banana in a small bowl with a fork
  5. sieve together the flour, baking powder and salt
  6. stir in the butter to the eggs while mixing
  7. add in the mashed banana and mix well
  8. add in the dry ingredients and fold in until lumps have been broken down
  9. divide into a muffin tray lined with cases
  10. sprinkle sesame seeds on top and bake for 20 minutes or until lightly golden
  11. cool completely and refrigerate​ before eating
