The ingredients needed to make Vanilla cup cakes:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoons salt
  4. Prepare 1 stick unsalted butter
  5. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  6. Prepare 3 eggs
  7. Make ready 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Get 3/4 cup milk
