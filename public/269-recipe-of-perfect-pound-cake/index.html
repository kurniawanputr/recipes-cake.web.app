The ingredients needed to make Pound cake:

  2. Make ready 6 eggs
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup melted butter
  4. Take 1 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
