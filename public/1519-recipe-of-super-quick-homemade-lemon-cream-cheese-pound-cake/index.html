The ingredients needed to make Lemon Cream Cheese Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready Grams Flour
  2. Take Corn flour
  3. Make ready Sugar
  4. Take Eggs
  5. Prepare Baking Soda
  6. Prepare Salt
  7. Prepare Butter
  8. Get Cream Cheese
  9. Make ready Vanilla Essence
  10. Take Lemon juice
  11. Take Lemon Rind
  12. Prepare Sugar
  13. Prepare Lemon Juice
Steps to make Lemon Cream Cheese Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 160 degrees. Butter and flour a 10 inch bundt pan. keep aside.
  2. Mix together flour, corn flour, salt and baking soda. Keep aside.
  3. Beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Add 450 gms sugar and beat the mixture for atleast 5 mins until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time and beat well after each addition.
  5. Mix in vanilla essence and lemon juice, followed by lemon rind.
  6. Fold in the flour mixture into the above mixture in three additions.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 1-1.5 hours.
  8. Cool the cake in the pan for 10 mins and then flip it out of the pan.
  9. Prepare the lemon syrup. For this, whisk together 100 gms of sugar and 60 ml lemon juice until sugar is dissolved.
  10. Generously brush the lemon syrup over the cooled cake.
  11. Sprinkle with granulated sugar and serve with strawberries.
