The ingredients needed to make Vanilla pound cake:
  1. Get 250 g selfraising flour
  2. Prepare 250 g sugar
  3. Prepare 250 g margarine/butter
  4. Get 50 ml milk
  5. Get 3 eggs
  6. Make ready 3 ml vanilla essence

Instructions to make Vanilla pound cake:
  1. Start by whisking the margarine and sugar together
  2. Beat the eggs in separate bowl then add them in the mixture and continue whisking
  3. Add the vanilla essence
  4. Sift the flour and add it in the mixture as you continue whisking
  5. Add milk
  6. When it's well mixed pour in the baking tin, pre heat oven
  7. Place in oven and let it bake in the given time
  8. Enjoy
