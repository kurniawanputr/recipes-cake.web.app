Instructions to make Carrot cake recipe:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
  2. Prepare the baking tin and set aside
  3. Measure all the dry ingredients and set aside.Mix the flour and baking powder and shift it.
  4. Put the vegetable oil in bowl,add caster sugar and mix throughly (it doesn't require a lot of engergy) Add eggs and continue mixing it.
  5. Start add in the flour slowly as you mix until it is mixed evenly. Then add grated carrots to it.and stir for a few minutes
  6. Pour the mixture into the baking tin
  7. Put in the oven to bake for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for 5 to 10 minutes.then transfer it to a plate.
  9. Serve with hot African tea.
