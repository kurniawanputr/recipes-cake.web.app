Instructions to make Lemon yoghurt pound cake:
  1. Measure your butter and let it melt using microwave or heating in a pan. Mix with lemon juice. Preheat your oven at 180 degrees C
  2. In a bowl put eggs and sugar. Start to beat egg with hand mixer
  3. When your egg becomes white and airy enough, stop beating
  4. Add flour and baking powder while sifting and fold them gently
  5. Add your butter, lemon juice and lemon zest. Mix them some times from the bottom.
  6. Add yoghurt and mix roughly until just incorporated.
  7. When your batter ready, pour it into your tin.
  8. Bake in a oven for 45 minutes at 180℃ (until a toothpick inserted in the middle of the cake comes out clean)
  9. It’s done!
