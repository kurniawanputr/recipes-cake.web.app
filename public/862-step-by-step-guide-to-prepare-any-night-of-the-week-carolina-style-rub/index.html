The ingredients needed to make Carolina style rub:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  2. Take 1/2 cup salt

  4. Make ready 1/4 cup black pepper
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup white pepper
  6. Take 1 1/2 tablespoons onion powder
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 tablespoons garlic powder
  8. Get 2 teaspoons ground mustard
