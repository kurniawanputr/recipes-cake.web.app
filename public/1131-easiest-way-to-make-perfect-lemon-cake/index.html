The ingredients needed to make Lemon Cake 🎂:
  1. Make ready Cake
  2. Get butter
  3. Make ready castor sugar
  4. Take extra large eggs
  5. Make ready flour
  6. Prepare baking powder
  7. Prepare salt
  8. Get milk
  9. Take lemon essence
  10. Prepare lemon juice
  11. Get Icing
  12. Prepare icing sugar
  13. Take Lemon juice
Instructions to make Lemon Cake 🎂:
  1. Pre heat oven 180 degrees
  2. Cream butter and castor sugar
  3. Add eggs one at a time
  4. Add dry ingredients and milk
  5. Add lemon essence and lemon juice
  6. Pour batter into well gressed pan
  7. Bake for 40 minutes or until skewer is clean
  8. Glaze- combine icing sugar with lemon juice
