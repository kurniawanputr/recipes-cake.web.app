Steps to make Mango cup cakes:

  2. Take all-purpose flour maida,add baking powder,baking soda and salt and mix it well.Take a bawl,add powder sugar,mango pulp,oil,and milk.mix it finely and slowly add one by one all-purpose flour maida in it.made the fine paste,if needed add milk for fine batter.
  3. Pour batter of cake in small cups.Take a kadhai,pre heat for 10 minutes,put 1 jali or plate any available in the home.took all cup cakes in it for 15 to 20 minutes.Look after 20 minutes with a knife for plate tasting,if it's not sticky then cake is ready.
