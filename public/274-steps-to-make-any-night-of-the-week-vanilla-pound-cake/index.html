The ingredients needed to make Vanilla pound cake:
  1. Prepare 2 cups flour
  2. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. Get 1 \2 cup buttermilk
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. Prepare 3 eggs
  7. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  8. Make ready 1 \2 cup butter
  9. Take 3 tablespoons veg. Oil

Steps to make Vanilla pound cake:
  1. Mix sugar and butter with a mixer till soft and fluffy.
  2. Add veg. Oil and buttermilk. Break the eggs and add to the batter.
  3. Sift flour and add baking soda and powder, then add to the first batter and mix. Then add the vanilla extract.
  4. Grease baking pan and dust with little flour. Scoop and put in the ban. Bake in a low heat.
