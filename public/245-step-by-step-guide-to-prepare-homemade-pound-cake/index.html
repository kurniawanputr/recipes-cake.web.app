The ingredients needed to make Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready 200 gm soft butter
  2. Get 50 gm brown sugar
  3. Prepare 4 room temp egg
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  5. Make ready 220 gm plain flour
  6. Make ready 3 tsp baking powder

Instructions to make Pound Cake:
  1. Mix butter with sugar, vanilla essence. Mix it until creamy.
  2. Add egg one at a time.
  3. Sift flour and baking powder. Add to the wet mixture. Fold it gently until mixed nicely.
  4. Put it in baking pan. 170°, bottom shelf, warm water on side. Bake it for 40 min.
