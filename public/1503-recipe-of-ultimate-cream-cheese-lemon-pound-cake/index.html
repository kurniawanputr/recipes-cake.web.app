The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Lemon Pound Cake:
  1. Prepare Cake
  2. Take Softened butter(1 and 1/2 cup)
  3. Make ready cream cheese, softened
  4. Make ready eggs
  5. Take lemon juice
  6. Prepare lemon extract
  7. Get granulated sugar
  8. Make ready all-purpose flour
  9. Make ready vanilla extract
  10. Get Glaze
  11. Get milk
  12. Prepare Glaze
  13. Get lemon juice
  14. Prepare powdered sugar
  15. Prepare lemon extract
Steps to make Cream Cheese Lemon Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat your oven to 325. Grease & flour bundt pan with shortening or butter. Place to the side.
  2. Allow butter, cream cheese, and eggs to come to room temperature. Cream together butter, cream cheese, and sugar till smooth and fluffy
  3. Add in eggs one at a time. Add in lemon juice and extract.
  4. Mix in Flour and vanilla extract just until combined. DO NOT OVERMIX!!
  5. Pour into prepared bundt pan. Bake for 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean. Ovens vary
  6. For Glaze: whisk together powdered sugar, lemon juice, extract, and milk. Allow cake to cool, then drizzle glaze over cake…
