The ingredients needed to make Rice and Meatballs Batch 2:
  1. Get beef meatballs see my recipes
  2. Get cooked jasmine rice
  3. Make ready mixed vegetables
  4. Prepare water
  5. Prepare kosher salt
  6. Make ready granulated garlic powder
  7. Make ready creamy Caesar salad dressing
  8. Make ready zesty Italian salad dressing
  9. Make ready peanut oil
Steps to make Rice and Meatballs Batch 2:
  1. Chop the vegetables, I used carrots, cauliflower, and broccoli. Get a pot hot and add oil. Stir-fry the vegetables for 7 minutes. Add the meatballs.
  2. After stirfrying the meatballs and vegetables for 10 minutes add the salad dressing. Stir in the dressing then stir in the cooked rice.
  3. Add water, salt, and garlic cover for 15 minutes, stir and cover again remove from heat allow to rest 12 minutes covered. Serve, I hope you enjoy!!!
