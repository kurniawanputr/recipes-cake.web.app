Steps to make Unga wa ndovu lemon cake:
  1. In a container, beat the eggs and put sugar. Whisk them together until the sugar dissolves
  2. After add lemon essence,salt and yeast and whisk
  3. Add cooking oil and whisk plus the milk…whisk all of them together for sometime then add your flour. Whisk them together until all are mixed well
  4. Take a bigger sufuria put the mawe tatu inside and place it on the charcoal burner to heat. Then take a smaller sufuria apply blue band in it then put a little flour and rotate it all over the sufuria
  5. Take your mixture and pour it in the smaller sufuria. Insert the small sufuria in the large sufuria and cover with a big lid. Then put some charcoal on top…not much to avoid cake from burning. The jiko should remain without charcoal. Within 40 minutes it will be ready.
