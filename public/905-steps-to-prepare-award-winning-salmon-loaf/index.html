The ingredients needed to make Salmon loaf:

  2. Get 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  3. Get 1/8 teaspoon pepper

  5. Make ready 2 egg yolks
  6. Get 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
  7. Take 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup hot milk
  9. Make ready 2 egg whites
  10. Make ready 2 tablespoons melted butter
