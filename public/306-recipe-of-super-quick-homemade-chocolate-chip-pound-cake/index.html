The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip pound cake:
  1. Take 1 yellow cake mix
  2. Get 1 8oz carton sour cream
  3. Get 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  4. Get 4 eggs
  5. Prepare 12 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
  6. Get 1 box of instant pudding

Instructions to make Chocolate chip pound cake:
  1. Mix all the ingredient in one big bowl
  2. Mix well with a mixture
  3. Spray your pound cake pan. ( I use Pam original spray )
  4. Put the mix in the pan and put the cake pan on a COLD oven dial to 300 and bake for 1 hour
  5. After an hour take your cake out and let it cool for about 5 mins ( by then it not all the way cool off but it's ok ) turn it upside down on a plate and get the cake out. And ENJOY!!!
