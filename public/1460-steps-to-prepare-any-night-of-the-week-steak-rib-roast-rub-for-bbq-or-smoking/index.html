The ingredients needed to make Steak/Rib/Roast Rub for BBQ or Smoking:
  1. Take Light Brown Sugar
  2. Make ready Chili Powder
  3. Take Garlic Powder
  4. Prepare Minced Onion
  5. Make ready Paprika
  6. Prepare Black Pepper
  7. Take Dried Parsley
  8. Make ready Himalayan Salt
  9. Prepare Ground Mustard Seed
  10. Take Cumin
  11. Prepare Ground Celery Seed
  12. Make ready Ground Coriander
  13. Get Cinnamon
Steps to make Steak/Rib/Roast Rub for BBQ or Smoking:
  1. Mix all spices together. Be sure to break up the brown sugar so it doesn’t clump.
  2. Add some oil or some soy sauce and rub it around the meat. Then sprinkle spice rub and massage in.
  3. Roast it, Smoke it, or use it to grill - Enjoy!
