The ingredients needed to make Taco Soup:
  1. Take ground, shredded or cubed meat*
  2. Take medium Onion, chopped
  3. Make ready Garlic, minced **
  4. Make ready can Diced Green Chiles, Do not drain
  5. Prepare can Corn*** Do not drain
  6. Prepare cans No Salt Added beans, variety****Do not drain
  7. Get Taco Seasoning, approx. 3 to 4 packets
  8. Take Notes
  9. Make ready Literally ANY meat or your favorite sub works for this!
  10. Get * Dude. Garlic is to taste always! Your preference!
  11. Prepare ** Try Fire Roasted or Fiesta Style Corn! Delish
  12. Prepare ***I use Dark Red, Light Red, Black and Pinto in mine.Colorful!
Steps to make Taco Soup:
  1. In Large Pot add Meat, brown until almost done, drain fat.
  2. Add Chopped Onion and Minced Garlic, stir occasionally until onions are cooked as ya like and meat is cooked through.
  3. Add Green Chiles, Corn, Beans. Add Taco Seasoning. Stir well. Bring to boil, turn off and serve.
  4. Top with cheese, sour cream, cilantro, hot sauce, Corn Chips, Cornbread, etc.
