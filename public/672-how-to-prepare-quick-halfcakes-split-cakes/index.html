Steps to make Halfcakes /Split Cakes:
  1. In a bowl add the flour, turmeric, baking powder, baking powder, cardamom and salt. Mix and set aside.
  2. In another bowl add the egg, oil, vanilla essence and sugar. Mix thoroughly using a whisk till its light and runny.
  3. Add this mixture to the flour mixture and knead to form a firm dough.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 mins.
  5. After 30 mins, take out the Dough and roll it out using your hands to form a long thick rod. Cut it into medium thick pieces.
  6. Design the shapes you like by using a knife, deep fry on low heat.
  7. Enjoy with some black tea
