The ingredients needed to make Air Fryer Strawberry Muffins:
  1. Make ready all-purpose flour
  2. Take baking powder
  3. Take fine salt
  4. Prepare large or 2 small eggs
  5. Make ready oil
  6. Make ready granulated sugar
  7. Get milk
  8. Prepare vanilla
  9. Get Strawberry jam
  10. Prepare fresh or frozen strawberries
Steps to make Air Fryer Strawberry Muffins:
  1. Preheat the air fryer for 5 min.
  2. Whisk the flour, baking powder and salt in a large bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk eggs, oil and sugar then add milk and vanilla. Mix good.
  4. Mix dry ingredients with wet and add the strawberry jam and strawberries. Stir until just combined.
  5. Divide batter evenly among muffin cups and sprinkle with sugar.
  6. Set the air fryer to 350 degrees and bake for 11-15 min. Insert a toothpick to test if they have finished cooking.
  7. Enjoy!
