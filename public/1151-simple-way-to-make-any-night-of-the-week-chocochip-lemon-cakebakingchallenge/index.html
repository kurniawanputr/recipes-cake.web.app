Instructions to make Chocochip Lemon Cake#bakingchallenge:
  1. Preheat oven @160°c. Line 2 tins 8" or 1 large tin
  2. In a bowl mix sugar & butter. Beat till light & fluffy
  3. In another bowl mix flour,baking powder,baking soda,salt. Sieve set aside
  4. Now add 1 egg at a time to the butter mixture, then the vanilla essence.Now add flour in 3 additions & milk in 2 additions, finishing with flour. Don't overmix.
  5. Now add the lemon juice,zest& mix. Then add the chocolate chips. Mix lightly
  6. Pour in prepared tins & bake for 1&1/2 hour or till done
  7. Once done, let it cool. Decorate with cream & chocolate decorations
