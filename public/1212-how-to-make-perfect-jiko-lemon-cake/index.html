Instructions to make Jiko lemon cake:
  1. Light your jiko
  2. Sieve your flour
  3. Grate the lemon rind and squeeze the lemon juice
  4. Mix the flour, baking powder, sugar and the grated lemon rind
  5. Rub the blueband to the mixture
  6. Make a well and add the eggs and mix well and add the water and cooking oil and the lemon juice to achieve a batter consistency
  7. Prepare your sufurias on a big sufuria place 4 spoons
  8. On the smaller one put your batter and place inside the big sufuria and place it on the jiko
  9. Put your lid and place a wet table cloth on the lid
  10. Bake for 30- 45 minutes
  11. Insert a squure if it comes out clean the cake is ready
