The ingredients needed to make Pfeffernusse Pound Cake:
  1. Prepare 1/2 lb butter (2 sticks) room temp
  2. Get 1/2 cup vegetable shortening (room temp)
  3. Make ready 3 cups sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  5. Prepare 5 eggs (room temp)
  6. Take 3 cups all-purpose flour
  7. Make ready 1 cup milk
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup molasses
  9. Take 1/4 cup honey
  10. Take 1 1/2 tsp cardomon
  11. Prepare 1 tsp nutmeg
  12. Make ready 1 tsp cloves
  13. Get 1 tsp ginger
  14. Get 2 tsp anise extract
  15. Take 2 tsp cinnamon
  16. Get 1 tsp pepper
  17. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  18. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  19. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  20. Make ready Powdered sugar
Instructions to make Pfeffernusse Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour bundt or spring form pan.
  2. With mixer cream butter and shortening together. Add sugar and brown sugar a little at a time. Add eggs, one at a time, beating after each addition.
  3. Stir dry ingredients together in a separate bowl (flour, cardomon, nutmeg, cloves, ginger, cinnamon, pepper, salt and baking powder). Add to butter mixture alternating with milk.
  4. Add in molasses, honey, anise and vanilla extracts.
  5. Pour batter into greased and floured tube/bundt pan. Bake for 1 - 1 1/2 hours until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. When cooled remove from pan and dust with powered sugar.
  7. Enjoy!
