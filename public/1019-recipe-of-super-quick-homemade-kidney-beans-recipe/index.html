The ingredients needed to make Kidney Beans Recipe:
  1. Prepare boiled kidney beans
  2. Take water
  3. Get tomato paste
  4. Prepare ginger garlic paste
  5. Get Rajma masala
  6. Prepare chili powder
  7. Get Salt as per taste
  8. Take Chopped coriander
Steps to make Kidney Beans Recipe:
  1. Take kidney beans in a bowl washed properly, and dip into the water for the whole night
  2. Next day, put kidney beans, water, and 1 tsp salt into the pressure cooker.put on the gas for 20 -25 minutes on the medium flame until its cook
  3. Heat oil in a pan, put chopped onion, ginger garlic paste. Then add tomato paste, chili, rajma masala,salt and stir for 5 minutes
  4. Add boiled beans with water and on gas for 5 minutes.
  5. Add chopped coriander leaves and its ready to serve. You can eat with rice
