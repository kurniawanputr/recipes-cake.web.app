The ingredients needed to make Pound Cake:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup buter
  2. Make ready 2 1/4 cup suger
  3. Make ready 5 eggs
  4. Get 1 tbsp vanilla
  5. Make ready 1 1/4 cup milk
  6. Get 3 cup flour
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions to make Pound Cake:
  1. preheat oven to 180ºC
  2. In a large bowl. cream together butter and suger until light and fluffy
  3. Beat in eggs then stir in the vanilla
  4. beat in the flour mixture and add milk
  5. pour batter into prepared pan
  6. bake in oven for 80 to 90 minutes. or until a toothpick inserted into the centre of the cake and comes out clean.
  7. let cool in the pan then turn it out
