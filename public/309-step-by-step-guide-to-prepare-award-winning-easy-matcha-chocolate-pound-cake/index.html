The ingredients needed to make Easy! Matcha Chocolate Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready 100 grams White flour
  2. Make ready 45 grams×3 pieces White chocolate
  3. Take 2 Eggs
  4. Get 2 tbsp Cream or milk
  5. Take 50 grams Margarine or butter
  6. Make ready 50 grams Sugar
  7. Make ready 10 grams Matcha
  8. Make ready 1 Powdered Sugar
Steps to make Easy! Matcha Chocolate Pound Cake:
  1. Measure the ingredients. Chop the chocolate and put it into a heatproof container with milk. Microwave for about 15 seconds.
  2. Bring the butter to room temperature. Cream the butter and sugar well. Add the beaten eggs and mix very well.
  3. Sift the flour and matcha in the bowl and mix.
  4. Add the dissolved chocolate and mix well.
  5. Line a pound cake mold with parchment paper and pour in the mixture. Bake in the oven preheated to 355F/180C, for about 30 minutes.
  6. Chill and serve. It's soft and chewy. The photo shows the cake with chocolate chips!
  7. I baked this cake with 80g flour and 15g matcha. It turned out a bit oily, though.
  8. This was chilled in the fridge. The matcha and chocolate flavor was very concentrated.
