The ingredients needed to make Dora cakes:
  1. Get 2 tbsp Butter
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp Cream
  3. Get 4 tbsp Condensed milk
  4. Get 1/2 cup Powder Sugar
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Water
  6. Get 1 tbsp Baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 Cup Maida (All purpose flour)
  8. Get 1 cup Chocolate spread

Steps to make Dora cakes:
  1. Take a bowl add butter,cream,condensed milk and sugar and mix it well with the whisk or fork till it get light in weight or for 4 minutes.
  2. Now add water in it and mix it well.Now add baking powder and maida in it and mix it to get a smooth consistency neither thick nor thin.
  3. Now take a pan whose having lid.ON the flame and heat the pan on low flame.Now with the help of spoon or any other small cup pour the mixture don't scattered it.It will get itself.Now cover the lid.
  4. Cook it till when u see bubbles on the upper side and flip it and cook other side also.Like this cook all the pancakes.
  5. Now take a chocolate spread or hershey syrup.Apply on the inner side of dora cake and cover it with other dora cake.Our yummy delicious dora cakes are ready to serve.
