The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cup cakes:
  1. Make ready 2 cup Maida
  2. Make ready 2 tsp Baking powder
  3. Get 1/2 cup Coco powder
  4. Make ready 1 cup Milk
  5. Prepare 1 cup Oil
  6. Make ready 1 cup Powered sugar
  7. Prepare 2 Eggs
  8. Take 1/2 tsp Vanila essence
  9. Make ready Choclate chips

Instructions to make Chocolate cup cakes:
  1. Firstly mix dry ingredients. (Without powered sugar).
  2. Beat 2 eggs. Add sugar in it and again beat it.
  3. Add oil,milk, vanila escence in it and beat it for a while.
  4. Then add dry ingredients and mix them with spatula.
  5. Now grease the tray with oil and put cupcakes clinkers on it. And pour 3 tbs of batter in each clinker.Sprinkle chocolate chips aur grated dark chocolate on it.
  6. Tap it 2 times to remove air bubbles.
  7. Now pre-heat the oven or pateela for 10min. Then put tray in oven and bake it at 220°C. For 15 min. In pateela bake it for 20-25 min on low-med flame.
  8. And it is ready to serve.
