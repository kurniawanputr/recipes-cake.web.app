The ingredients needed to make Lavender Dumplings - For Chicken Broth:
  1. Make ready 1/2 Cup AP Flour
  2. Get 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  3. Make ready Large pinch Salt
  4. Take 70 ml Milk
  5. Take 1/4 Teaspoon Dry Lavender Tea

Steps to make Lavender Dumplings - For Chicken Broth:
  1. Mix everything together in a bowl then make into 6 balls with floured hands
  2. Let to sit for 3-5 mins then carefully drop into broth and cover with lid
  3. After 8 mins lift the lid and test the dumpling with a toothpick which should come out clean - if not cover and cook for another 2 mins.
  4. Serve and Enjoy :)
