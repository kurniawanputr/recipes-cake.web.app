Instructions to make Lamington Cake

Recipe by post from Australia, Emily For coco lovers!:

  1. The recipe is simple like a cake.
  2. Mix the sugar and the butter until they ll be white and creamy. Add the eggs. Turn off the mixer and add the vanilla, the flour and the baking powder
  3. Bake in 180 degres for 20-30 min. Leave it and when is cold cut in pieces if you like.
  4. Put in a bowl all the ingridients for the icing and put the bowl on the top of boil water (bath), until is a bit solid.
  5. Icing the cake or the pieces of the cake.
  6. Roll them into coconut and let it rest for a while!
  7. Enjoy! you can serve it with whipped cream!
