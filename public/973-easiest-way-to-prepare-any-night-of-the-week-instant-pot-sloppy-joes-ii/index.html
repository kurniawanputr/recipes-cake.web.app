Steps to make Instant pot sloppy joes (II):
  1. Place the instant pot on Saute setting. Brown the ground beef and drain any excess grease. - Cancel the saute setting. Add in the mustard, brown sugar, ketchup and water. Stir. - Place the lid on the top and make sure the lid is set to sealing. - Set the pressure to high for 5 minutes. - You can do a quick release.
