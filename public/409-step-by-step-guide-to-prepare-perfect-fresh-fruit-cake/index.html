Step-by-Step Guide to Prepare Perfect Fresh Fruit Cake

The ingredients needed to make Fresh Fruit Cake:
  1. Get Sunflower / Canola / Grapeseed or any other Neutral Oil, 118g + More For Greasing
  2. Take Eggs,
  3. Get Demerara Sugar,
  4. Make ready Dark Muscovado Sugar,
  5. Make ready Unbleached All Purpose Flour,
  6. Take Sea Salt,
  7. Take Chinese 5 Spice Powder, 1/2 TSP
  8. Take Baking Soda,
  9. Get Baking Powder,
  10. Make ready Fresh Fruits Coarsely Diced (I am using Nectarines & Raspberries),
  11. Take Icing Sugar, For Dusting
Steps to make Fresh Fruit Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 175 degree celsius or 350 fahrenheit. - - Grease the cake pan with oil and lined with parchment paper (on the bottom and at the sides as well). - - Transfer eggs and sugar into a large mixing bowl. - - Using a hand or stand mixer, beat for 7 mins or until tripled in volume.
  2. Add in oil and continue beating until well combined. - - In another bowl, mix together flour, salt, 5 spice, baking powder and soda. - - Fold the flour mixture into the egg mixture, 1/3 portion at a time. - - Lastly, fold in the fresh fruits. - - Once everything is incorporated, transfer the batter into the prepped cake pan.
  3. Wack into the oven and bake for 50 to 60 mins or until it passes the skewer test. - - It should puff up a bit and dark brown in color. - - Remove from the oven and set aside to cool down completely. - - Once cooled, unmold onto a serving plate or cake stand. - - Lightly dust with icing sugar. - - You can slice and serve it warm or chilled. I prefer mine warm.
