The ingredients needed to make Scottyboy's Pain Cakes:
  1. Make ready 1 cup milk
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup oil
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Take 2 cups flour
  5. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp Baking powder
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Salt
  8. Get Splash vanilla
  9. Prepare 1 cup berries
Instructions to make Scottyboy's Pain Cakes:
  1. Mix wet and dry ingredients separately, then combine. Don't over stir or they become flat.
  2. Add berries if desired
  3. Cook on buttered griddle
