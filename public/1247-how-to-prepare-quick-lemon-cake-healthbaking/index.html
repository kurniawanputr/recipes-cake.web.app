Steps to make Lemon cake #Healthbaking#:
  1. Grease your baking tin.
  2. Sieve your flour.
  3. In a clean mixing bowl, put your sugar n blueband n mix them completely till they both incoporate.
  4. Add eggs one by one as you mix.
  5. Grate your lemon and squeeze its juice then add it to your mixture.
  6. Fold in your flour into the mixture. Avoid over mixing.
  7. Put it in your oven at 160 degrees celsius or bake using mawe tatu method.
  8. Bake for 45 mins or till its ready or fully baked.
  9. Enjoy your lemon cake with a drink of your choice…Happy #healthbaking#.
