The ingredients needed to make Lemon pound cake with lemon glaze:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Get 4 tbs squeezed lemon juice
  3. Get 3/4 cup oil
  4. Take 1 cup castor sugar
  5. Prepare 4 eggs
  6. Get 1 Tbs baking powder
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup corn starch
  9. Prepare 2/3 cup milk
  10. Get I can sweet condensed cream
  11. Make ready 1/3 cup lemon juice

Instructions to make Lemon pound cake with lemon glaze:
  1. Beat eggs and separate the egg white from the yolk
  2. Whip the egg white for 5 minutes and set aside
  3. In a separate bowl mix the egg yolk,salt,oil and sugar to form a thick paste
  4. Add milk to the mixture and the 5tbs of lemon and mix
  5. Sieve the two (corn & wheat) flours and baking powder and add to the mixture while mixing to form a thick paste.
  6. Add the egg white to the mixture and mix.
  7. Pour the mixture to the baking tin and place it in a preheated oven for 45minutes at 180 degrees.
  8. Mix the sweet condensed cream with 1/3 cup of lemon juice and refrigerate for 1hr.
  9. Apply the lemon glaze on the cake and refrigerate for 30 mins
