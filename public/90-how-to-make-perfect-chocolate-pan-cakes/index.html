The ingredients needed to make Chocolate pan cakes:
  1. Make ready 1 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  5. Get 1 tbsp powdered milk
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Prepare 1 tspn baking powder
  8. Get Pinch salt
  9. Prepare Grated coconut
  10. Get Dark chocolate, melted
  11. Take White chocolate
  12. Prepare pieces Chocolate chips broken into small
  13. Prepare 1/3 cup coco powder

Instructions to make Chocolate pan cakes:
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, coco powder, milk, oil nd water to create a light batter.
  2. Heat ur non stick frying pan over a medium heat. Add a little oil or butter nd use kitchen paper to smear it all over the base of the pan.
  3. Add nd swirl the mixture around the pan nd cook for 1- 2 mins on each side over a medium heat. Remove the pancake nd repeat with the other the batter.
  4. Place all the pancakes onto a plate nd filled them with the melted chocolate nd white chocolate. Add some grated coconut nd chocolate chips. Served !
