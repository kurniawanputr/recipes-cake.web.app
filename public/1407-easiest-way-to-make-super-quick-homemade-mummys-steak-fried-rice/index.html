The ingredients needed to make Mummy’s Steak Fried Rice:
  1. Take cooked steak, diced
  2. Prepare cooked rice
  3. Prepare soy sauce
  4. Get garlic, minced
  5. Prepare olive oil
Steps to make Mummy’s Steak Fried Rice:
  1. In a frying pan, stir fry the minced garlic with the olive oil over medium heat, until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced steak, already cooked. Then, add the soy sauce and stir fry for a bit before adding the rice. Stir fry for approximately another minute.
  3. Season with salt and pepper accordingly, and optionally garnish with spring onion.
