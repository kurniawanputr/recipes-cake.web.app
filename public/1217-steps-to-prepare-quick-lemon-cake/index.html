Steps to make Lemon Cake:
  1. Zest the lemon.
  2. Mix the egg yolks, oil, lemon zest and lemon juice in a bowl.
  3. Juice the lemon and simmerit on medium heat for 15 minutes, after 15 minutes you shoud be left whith 1 tablespoons of juice.
  4. Whip the egg whithes whith the sugar a 1/4 at a time until is like a merengue.
  5. Fold the egg yolk mixture in the egg whites.
  6. Sift the flour, salt and baking powder and fold 1/4 at a time.
  7. Divide the mixture evenly between 2 loaf tins and bake at 180°C for 40 to 45 minutes.
  8. Mix the icing sugar and lemon juice from one lemon and pour it over the hot cake.
  9. Enjoy whith a glass of milk.
