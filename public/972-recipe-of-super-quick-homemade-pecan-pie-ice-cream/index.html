The ingredients needed to make Pecan pie ice cream:
  1. Get brown sugar
  2. Prepare dark corn syrup
  3. Get butter
  4. Prepare eggs
  5. Get heavy cream
  6. Get salt
  7. Prepare pecans (can omit if you want to)
  8. Take heavy cream (to taste)
  9. Take milk (to taste)

Steps to make Pecan pie ice cream:
  1. Mix in small pot the brown sugar, corn syrup, and butter. Simmer for 2 min and let cool
  2. Mix in bowl the eggs, salt, and 1/4 cup heavy cream
  3. Mix together when cool then add heavy cream and milk to taste. Will fill up 1-2 wonton containers probably. Freeze in chest freezer for 6 hours or more
