Instructions to make Lemon tea cake:
  1. Mix the lemon juice with milk, after 10 minutes add butter to turn the mixture into buttermilk
  2. Next add the mixture with flour, icing sugar, baking soda and baking powder, salt, lemon zest & fresh lemon juice..
  3. Take a square shape bowl & grease with butter and pour the batter in the bowl and tap it 3 times so the mixture settles in properly
  4. Preheat the oven for 10 minutes at 180°C and bake for 25-30 minutes..
  5. To make Glaze top.. Mix 6 tbsp icing sugar, 1tbsp lemon juice and 1+1/2 tbsp water..
  6. When the cake is baked, cool it. Then pour the mixture for the glaze top..
