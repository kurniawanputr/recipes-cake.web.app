The ingredients needed to make Thai Fish Cakes:
  1. Take 250 grams Haddock
  2. Get 1 large egg
  3. Prepare Pinch salt (optional)
  4. Prepare 45 grams red Thai curry paste
  5. Make ready 5 green beans
  6. Make ready 2 spring onion
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon chopped green chilli
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  9. Get 3 tablespoons coriander leaves

Instructions to make Thai Fish Cakes:
  1. Cut the fish into chunks and dry with paper towel.
  2. Thinly cut the Green Beans and Spring Onion (about half a cm) and set aside in a bowl. This will be used in a later step
  3. Put everything apart from the Green Beans and Spring Onions into a food processor and turn it on until it is minced to a fine texture. Do not blend it to a paste.
  4. Transfer to a bowl and mix in the spring onion and the green beans
  5. Create your fishcakes and wrap in cling film. You should be able to make 8 reasonable sized fishcakes.
  6. Put the fishcakes into the fridge for about an hour to firm up.
  7. Put about a cm of sunflower oil into a pan and cook the fishcakes until dark golden brown on both sides.
