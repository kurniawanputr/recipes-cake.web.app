Steps to make Holmstead Cookin': Baked Onion & Garlic Scalloped Potates:
  1. Peel and slice potatoes into thin slices.
  2. Dice onion into small pieces, and fry onion, minced garlic, and vegetable oil in a saucepan (medium-low heat)
  3. Once the onions have started to caramelize, add 1 liter of heavy cream. Simmer and reduce until thickened.
  4. Layer the potatoes in a baking pan, adding the thickened sauce and salt/pepper on each layer.
  5. Cover and bake at 400 for 45 minutes. Then uncover, and bake for a further 30 minutes to get a golden brown top layer.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
