The ingredients needed to make Eggless Chocolate Banana Pound Cake Recipe:
  1. Get All purpose flour
  2. Prepare sugar Powdered
  3. Take Cocoa
  4. Get Baking Powder
  5. Take Robusta , ripe
  6. Make ready Vanilla Essence
  7. Make ready Milk (enough to form the smooth mix)
  8. Get Butter
Instructions to make Eggless Chocolate Banana Pound Cake Recipe:
  1. Make a puree of Robust by boiling it and mix with a teaspoon baking powder-This becomes a substitute for Egg.Preheat the Oven to 180 degree C or 350 degree F
  2. Sieve All Purpose flour, Powdered Sugar,Baking Powder (1 Teaspoon),Cocoa Powder and Repeat this 3 times.
  3. Mix half cup sugar and melted butter and make into smooth paste and add the robusta-baking soda mix into it.
  4. Next put this mix in a bowl and slowly add flour mix step by step,Add milk to attain the desired consistency.Repeat the process till drop consistency is achieved
  5. Take the baking pan -rectangle of round,apply butter on the walls and the base.
  6. Add the smooth cake mix into it and if required top it with roasted cashews.Bake this for about 45 mins.
