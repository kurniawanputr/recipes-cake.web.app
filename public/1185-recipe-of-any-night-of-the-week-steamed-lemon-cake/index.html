Steps to make Steamed lemon cake:
  1. Boil water in a large pan and insert small bowl inside. Grease your baking tin.
  2. Mix eggs and sugar then add water.
  3. Sieve flour with baking powder and mix evenly.
  4. Add vegetable oil and the essence then mix.
  5. Pour your batter in the baking tin.
  6. Place tin on top of small bowl inside large pan then cover with clean table cloth.
  7. Steam for 40mins. Then serve with tea.
