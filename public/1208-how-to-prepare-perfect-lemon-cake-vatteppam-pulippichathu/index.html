Instructions to make Lemon Cake (Vatteppam pulippichathu):
  1. Mix the rice flour and wheat flour and add a little water to make a batter
  2. Make sure the batter is of liquid consistency
  3. Add in the lemon juice, oil sugar, salt and baking powder
  4. Pour into mould and steam on a perforated vessel with adequate water underneath.
  5. Keep the cooking on low flame for 15 minutes till the steamed lemon cake has risen.
  6. This can be served at tea time.
