The ingredients needed to make Pound cake:
  1. Get 200 grams flour
  2. Make ready 200 grams sugar
  3. Get 4 eggs (200 gram)
  4. Get 5 grams baking powder
  5. Take 2 grams salt
  6. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence optional
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp tutti fruity
  8. Prepare 150 grams butter
  9. Take 2 tbsp milk
  10. Make ready 1/2 lemon jiuce

Instructions to make Pound cake:
  1. Take the butter. Beat the butter in room temperature till soft.
  2. Now add sugar and beat again till soft and fluffy.Then add milk and beat again.
  3. After this add egg one by one beat nicely.(For beating you can use hand whisker or your hand also.)
  4. Now sieve all dry ingredients.And just give a nice mix with spatula.(careful no need to beat)
  5. Add lemon juice and tutti fruity.And mix it well (no lumps).
  6. Transfer in mould.Bake it for 50 to 55 minute.In 170 °C (pre heated oven)Then after baking grease some butter for good taste and upper softness.
  7. D mold it. Cut in slices and keep in cling wrap for more softening. This is the way you make marry cake at home.
