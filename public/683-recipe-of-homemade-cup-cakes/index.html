Instructions to make Cup Cakes:
  1. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  2. Add eggs 1 at a time beating well between each addition
  3. Add vanilla essence and beat
  4. Add flour, milk and baking powder and mix well
  5. Fill mixture in cup cake holders until half full
  6. Bake at 180 degrees for 20 minutes check cuppies are done by inserting a toothpick which comes out clean
  7. Decorate as desired
