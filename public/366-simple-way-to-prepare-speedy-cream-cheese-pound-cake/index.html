The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready unsalted butter ( room temperature
  2. Take Cream Cheese ( room temperature
  3. Make ready Plain Flour
  4. Get Sugar
  5. Take Baking Powder
  6. Get Brown Eggs ( room temperature
  7. Make ready salt
  8. Make ready Vanilla extract
Instructions to make Cream Cheese Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Mix cream cheese and butter in a bowl until fluffy
  3. Add sugar
  4. Mix in eggs one at a time
  5. In a separate bowl whisk flour, salt and baking powder. Once done combine mixtures..Mix well
  6. Grease and flour Bundt cake pan or you can use baking spray
  7. Pour cake batter into pan. Allow to cook 1 hr & 25-30 min depending bon your oven.
