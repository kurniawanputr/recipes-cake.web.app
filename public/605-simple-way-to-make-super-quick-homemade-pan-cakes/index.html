The ingredients needed to make Pan Cakes:
  1. Take mandazi flour
  2. Make ready milk powder
  3. Prepare eggs
  4. Get sugar adjust according to your liking
  5. Get oat flour
  6. Get Milk
  7. Take muesli(nuts,raisins,almonds)
Instructions to make Pan Cakes:
  1. I mixed eggs and sugar using a mixer
  2. Dry ingredients mandazi flour,milk powder and oat flour and sieve together
  3. Mix eggs mix and dry ingredients,add milk to your liking and get a thick paste
  4. I used a double pan to do the pancakes
  5. I added some roasted muesli which i didnt want to spoil to the pancake dough
