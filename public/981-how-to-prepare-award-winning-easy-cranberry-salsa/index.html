The ingredients needed to make Easy Cranberry Salsa:
  1. Prepare 14 oz can whole berry cranberry sauce
  2. Take 1/2 cup fine chopped cilantro (or 1tbsp of dried)
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup fine diced red onion (or sweet white)
  4. Take 1 1/4 tbsp olive oil
  5. Make ready 3/4 tspn kosher salt
  6. Prepare 3/4 tspn cumin powder
  7. Get 3/4 tspn fine mixed jalapeno pepper
  8. Take 1/2 tspn coarse ground black pepper
  9. Prepare 2 dashes mild hot sauce
