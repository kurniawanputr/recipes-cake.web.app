The ingredients needed to make Almond flour cake:
  1. Take 4 oz(half a Philly bar or 120g) cream cheese room temp
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup sour cream
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup unsalted butter room temp
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup granulated Stevia
  5. Make ready 4 large room temp eggs
  6. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  8. Make ready 212 g blanched almond flour
  9. Take 2 teaspoons baking powder
  10. Make ready Pinch salt

Instructions to make Almond flour cake:
  1. Mix the butter, cream cheese, eggs, sour cream, extracts, and stevia together in a bowl
  2. Sift the almond flour baking powder together in separate bowl and mix
  3. Add bowls together and mix (have zero fear of over mixing there is no glutens)
  4. Pour in loaf pan that is lined with parchment because your lazy and cleaning loaf pans is annoying
  5. Bake at 340 for 40-50 min, test with toothpick
  6. Remove from oven, let cool. Set out on plate. Sift top with powdered sweeter, make a icing, or leave as is.. Up to you its your pound cake.
