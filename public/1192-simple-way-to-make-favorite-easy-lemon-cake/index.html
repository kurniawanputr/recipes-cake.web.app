The ingredients needed to make Easy Lemon Cake:
  1. Get cake
  2. Prepare white cake mix-follow mixing directions on the box
  3. Make ready lemon instant pudding
  4. Take pure lemon extract
  5. Prepare lemon juice
  6. Prepare lemon frosting-divided
Instructions to make Easy Lemon Cake:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. mix cake mix and all ingredients that's on the box, instant pudding, lemon extract, and 1/2 can of lemon frosting.
  3. pour into a greased and floured baking pan
  4. bake for 35 to 45 minutes
  5. after cooled. take the left over frosting and melt in microwave for 10-15 seconds
  6. drizzle over cake
