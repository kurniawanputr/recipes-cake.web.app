The ingredients needed to make Grandma Betty's Famous Pound Cake:
  1. Take 1 box Duncan Hines yellow cake mix
  2. Make ready 1 box (3.4 oz)jello instant Vanilla pudding
  3. Take 4 eggs
  4. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  5. Prepare 1 cup hot water
Steps to make Grandma Betty's Famous Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix all of the ingredients. Start off mixing at a slow speed, then you may increase the speed if you like. Mix for 4 minutes.
  3. Bake for 45 mins.
