Steps to make Lemon cake recipe:
  1. Cream the margarine and sugar.
  2. Beat the eggs and the essence in one bowl
  3. Mix the creamed margarine and the beaten eggs
  4. Onto the mixture add the mixed dry ingredients
  5. Mix it all up and put it in a greased baking tin
  6. Bake it in a preheated oven at 180°c for 50 minutes.
  7. For the cupcakes…..wait for them to cool before creaming…apply whipped cream using your preferred nozzle…. pour sprinkles to add onto the decor.
  8. For the cake even up the top….divide it into two and apply cream between the layer…
  9. Put the other layer on the cream and then apply cream to the whole cake
  10. Use a serrated knife to form the lines…then use different colour of cream to do decor
