Steps to make Blended Carrot Cake Recipe:
  1. Add 2 cups of flour to a bowl
  2. Add 2 table spoons of baking powder
  3. Add 1cup of sugar and a pinch of salt and mix well
  4. On the blender add 11/2 cup of grated carrot,two whole eggs and 1 cup oil then blend together
  5. Mixture should be this nice golden-orange color
  6. Add the mixture to the mixed dry ingredients on the bowl and mix well using a wooden spoon
  7. Grate the orange zest into the mixture.(I love it coz it eliminates the taste of raw eggs from cake)
  8. The whole mixture should be this tangerine color with a smooth consistency
  9. Prepare your baking tin with margarine and flour then bake the cake for 45 mins at 180 degrees until it browns well
