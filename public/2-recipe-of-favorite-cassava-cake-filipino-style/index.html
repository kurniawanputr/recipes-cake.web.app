The ingredients needed to make Cassava Cake (Filipino Style):
  1. Prepare 1 package grated frozen casava
  2. Get 2 egg yolk
  3. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Make ready 1 stick melted butter
  5. Make ready 1 bottle coconut macapuno strings)
  6. Prepare 1 can coconut milk
  7. Make ready 1 can evaporated milk
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup sugar
  9. Take 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)

Most of the ingredients can be found in an oriental store near you. You'll love this Filipino Style Cassava Cake. If you've never had it, you're in for a real treat. Seriously this lush treat is held in higher regard than the multitude of other local rice cakes or puddings in the Philippines.

Instructions to make Cassava Cake (Filipino Style):
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Grease a 9 by 13 pan
  3. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Mix well.
  4. Bake for 1 to 1 1/2 hours until top of cake turns golden brown. You can also brown the top by broiling it for roughly 1-2 minutes at the end of the baking process. If cake does not turn golden on top. Serve warm or at room temperature. Store leftovers in fridge.
