The ingredients needed to make Ma's Pound Cake:
  1. Get 3 cup sugar
  2. Get 1 cup butter
  3. Make ready 6 large eggs
  4. Get 1 tbsp vanilla
  5. Take 1 cup sour cream or milk
  6. Make ready 3 cup Cake Flour
Instructions to make Ma's Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Cream butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and beat until fluffy and stiff.
  4. Add vanilla
  5. Gradually add flour until thoroughly blended.
  6. Add in sour cream or milk and blend until light and fluffy
  7. Pour in greased tube pan
  8. Bake for 1 hour 45 mins do not open the door.
