The ingredients needed to make Paul's Thanksgiving Chopped Spinach:
  1. Take Wonder Bread or other crappy white bread
  2. Take frozen leaf spinach
  3. Get sour cream
  4. Prepare garlic
  5. Get butter
  6. Prepare cayenne pepper
  7. Make ready several grinds of fresh nutmeg
  8. Get salt and pepper
Instructions to make Paul's Thanksgiving Chopped Spinach:
  1. I cut off the crust and sometimes dry overnight first, then cut into croutons size with a serrated knife.
  2. In a small skillet, melt 2 Tbs+butter and add a crushed garlic clove.
  3. After the galic has sweat, add the bread and toss until browned. Set aside.
  4. Thaw and squeeze out excess moisture from the spinach then roughly chop.
  5. Melt the rest of the butter and saute the spinach.
  6. Add some cayenne, nutmeg and salt and pepper, all to taste.
  7. When it tastes right, set aside until just before serving.
  8. When ready to serve, reheat the spinach.
  9. Then add some sour cream, how much is up to you.
  10. Finish by stirring in the croutons.
  11. Serve hot/warm.
