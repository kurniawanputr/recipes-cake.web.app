The ingredients needed to make Sunshine's Lemon supreme pound cake:
  1. Get lemon cake mix
  2. Prepare sm lemon instant pudding mix
  3. Get crisco oil or vegetable oil
  4. Take water
  5. Prepare large eggs
  6. Get glaze
  7. Get powdered sugar
  8. Prepare lemon juice
Instructions to make Sunshine's Lemon supreme pound cake:
  1. Preheat oven at 350°„ bake At 350°.grease a tube pan .
  2. Blend ingredients in a large mixing bowl ,mix 2 minutes on medium speed, turn batter into tube pan, bake for 50 to 55 minutes .or until center springs back.
  3. Let it cool , then remove from pan .
  4. When it is completely cool down, mix sugar and lemon juice , spread on top. Enjoy
