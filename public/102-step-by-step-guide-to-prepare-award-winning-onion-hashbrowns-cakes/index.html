The ingredients needed to make Onion Hashbrowns/Cakes:
  1. Make ready 3 tablespoon cornstarch
  2. Make ready 2 large onions
  3. Take 1 large egg
  4. Make ready 1-1/4 cup evaporated milk
  5. Get 1-1/2 cup all purpose flour
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon seasoned salt
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  8. Get As needed oil to fry in
Instructions to make Onion Hashbrowns/Cakes:
  1. Slice the onion then slice the slices in half. Separate the pieces and add the cornstarch.
  2. Toss well coating every part of the onion pieces. Put into a ziploc bag for 15 minutes or more. Add seasoned salt.
  3. Mix the milk, salt, egg, and flour
  4. Add the onion and let rest in the batter for about 30-40 minutes.
  5. Fry in the oil turning as needed. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive amounts of oil.
  6. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
