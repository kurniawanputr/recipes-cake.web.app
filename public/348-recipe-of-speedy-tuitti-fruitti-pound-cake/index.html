The ingredients needed to make Tuitti Fruitti pound cake:
  1. Make ready 120 gms maida
  2. Take 60 gms butter
  3. Prepare 200 gms curd
  4. Take 150 gms sugar
  5. Take 1 tbsp white vinegar
  6. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 50 ml milk
  9. Take 1 tsp vanilla essence
  10. Take 1/2 cup tutti fruitti
Instructions to make Tuitti Fruitti pound cake:
  1. Grease a baking mould & line it with parchment paper, preheat the oven to 180°c.
  2. Sieve maida with baking powder & keep aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl take butter, add sugar & beat well for 5 mins, then add curd & again mix well, then add vanilla essence & mix well.
  4. Add vinegar to baking soda & add this mix to the butter mixture & mix well, then add maida & milk & make a nice batter.
  5. Lastly fold in tutti fruitti & pour the batter into the baking mould sprinkle some tutti Fruitti over it & bake it in a preheated oven for 35min.
  6. Let the cake cool for 2-3 hours & then slice it
