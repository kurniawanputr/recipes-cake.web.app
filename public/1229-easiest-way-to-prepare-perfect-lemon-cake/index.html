Instructions to make Lemon cake:
  1. In a bowl,grate Lemon grind,put the sugar and butter then beat until fluffy and light
  2. Beat the eggs and add the to the mixture above and mix
  3. Sieve your flour and baking powder then add bit by bit to the above mixture and mix,when it gets too thick add milk/water little by little…add your flavor and color at this point
  4. Do this till the flour mixture is over
  5. When your get your desired batter,bake it for 45-50minutes at 180°c
  6. Enjoy!
