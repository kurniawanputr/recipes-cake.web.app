Instructions to make Simple lemon cake:
  1. Pre heat your oven at 180d
  2. Grease your tin I used round 1 kg
  3. Mix sugar margarine and vegetable oil until pale.
  4. Break one egg at a time and mix at an interval of 1 minute per egg.
  5. Add vanilla lemon zest and juice and mix in one direction.
  6. Add sieved flour + baking powder. I sieved mine twice. Use a spatula to fold the batter until well incorporated. Dont forget to add the buttermilk.
  7. Pour in a pan then take a wet kitchen towel and tie around the tin
  8. Bake for 40 mins or use the toothpick method to check if it is done.
  9. Enjoy with or without frosting.
