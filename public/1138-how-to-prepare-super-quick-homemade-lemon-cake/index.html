Instructions to make Lemon Cake:
  1. Whisk eggs with sugar. Add other ingredients and whisk occasionally, in the order given above. Pour into a well-greased mold. (In my case, I used a silicone mold, so I didn't grease it.)
  2. Heat the oven.
  3. Bake for 30-40 minutes in 170 Celcius degrees.
  4. Cool for about 20-30 minutes before slicing and serving.
