The ingredients needed to make Amagwinya(Fat cakes):
  1. Take 6 cups flour
  2. Get 1 sachet yeast
  3. Take Pinch salt
  4. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Prepare Luke warm water
  6. Take Oil for frying

Instructions to make Amagwinya(Fat cakes):
  1. Sift all the dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add water bit by bit and mix until all the ingredients have combined (the dough must not be too wet)
  3. Leave the mixture in a warm place for the dough to rise or double in size
  4. Cut the dough into your desired size or shape and fry until golden brown
  5. Serve with your desired filling (mince,French, etc)
