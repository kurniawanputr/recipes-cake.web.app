Instructions to make Yummy red velvet cake recipe:
  1. In a large bowl sift flour and cocoa powder together, add sugar and whisk together, set aside
  2. In another bowl mix the vegetable oil, eggs, buttermilk and red color
  3. Mix the wet ingredients and dry ingredients until well combined
  4. In a small cup mix vinegar and baking soda and pour into the cake mixture
  5. Pour into cupcake liners and bake for 15 mins
