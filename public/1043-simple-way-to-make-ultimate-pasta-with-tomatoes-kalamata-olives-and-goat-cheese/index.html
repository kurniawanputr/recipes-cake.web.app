The ingredients needed to make Pasta with Tomatoes, Kalamata Olives, and Goat Cheese:
  1. Get pine nuts
  2. Get Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  3. Take garlic, minced
  4. Make ready crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  5. Prepare Plum Tomatoes, Diced
  6. Get kalamata olives, pitted, coarsely chopped
  7. Take spaghetti
  8. Get dried fresh basil
  9. Make ready About an inch of a small tube goat cheese
Instructions to make Pasta with Tomatoes, Kalamata Olives, and Goat Cheese:
  1. Directions - Heat a large skillet over medium heat, add the pine nuts and cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove and put on a plate to cool.
  2. Combine the oil, garlic, and red pepper in the skillet over medium heat until garlic is very fragrant, but not browned, about 2 minutes. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer until slightly thickened, about 15 minutes. Keep warm.
  3. Cook the Pasta
  4. Crumble the Goat Cheese, add to the sauce and stir in.
  5. Add the olives and pine nuts and cook until heated through Serve.
