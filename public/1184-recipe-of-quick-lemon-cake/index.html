Instructions to make Lemon cake:
  1. Mix butter till fluffy
  2. Add the oil and mix till smooth then add the sugar gradually until fluffy
  3. Mix well. Add the egg one at a time mix
  4. Add 1 cup of flour followed by half cup of milk, mix continue with the process until done
  5. Then lastly add the lemon juice and fold in the batter.pour the mixture into a grease pan and bake for 45minutes
  6. For the glaze, add icing sugar in a bowl then add lemon juice,
  7. Mix and add pinch of salt
  8. Drizzle some lemon juice over slice of cake and enjoy
