The ingredients needed to make Pound cake:
  1. Get 250 unsalted butter
  2. Make ready Flour 230 all purpose flour
  3. Get 20-30 g Sugar
  4. Get 5 eggs
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Get 50 g milk
  7. Make ready Half tsp of baking powder

Steps to make Pound cake:
  1. Rub a small amount of butter into the baking pan and set aside.
  2. Sieve the flour properly and mix with baking powder, set aside.
  3. Put the butter into a mixing bowl and with the help of a mixer, let it mix for about 2-3 minutes
  4. Pour the sugar into the butter and continue to mix for another five minutes
  5. Add the eggs and continue mixing
  6. Put the milk into the mixture and let it mix for another five minutes
  7. Add the vanilla extract into the mixture and mix well
  8. Start add the flour, one spoon at a time until all the is finish to form a batter
  9. Bake for about 30-40 minutes
