Instructions to make Lemon cake:
  1. Grease baking tin. In a bowl beat the margarine and sugar till fluffy and pale
  2. Add an egg at a time mixing ensure well incorporated
  3. Sift the dry ingredients and add slowly to the bowl mixing and adding sour milk little at a time. Add the rind and lemon juice with essence folding properly till right consistency is obtained
  4. Preheat oven at 180 degrees and bake batter for 50min till golden brown or with toothpick inserted at the centre comes out clean. Let cool for 15min in tin then invert for further cooling
