The ingredients needed to make Pancakes:
  1. Prepare 2 cup flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup milk
  3. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 2 eggs
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp orange zest
  6. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 1 pinch salt
  8. Get 4 tbsp oil
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Instructions to make Pancakes:
  1. At first mix egg & sugar until sugar dissolves
  2. Then add oil, milk, orange zest in the sugar mixture & mix well & add baking powder, salt, flour, mix properly. The batter will have medium consistency.
  3. Heat a pan in medium flame, pour 1 tbsp of batter in the pan as shown below. After about a minute, flip it so that the other side gets cooked. When both the sides are cooked properly remove it from heat sauce.
  4. And your delicious Pancakes are ready to serve! Serve them with honey or with fruits!
