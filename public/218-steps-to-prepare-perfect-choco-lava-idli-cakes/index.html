The ingredients needed to make Choco Lava idli Cakes:
  1. Make ready 1 Packet Bourbon Biscuits
  2. Make ready 250 ml Milk
  3. Prepare as needed oil
  4. Take 1/4 cup Dark Chocolates
  5. Make ready 1 Spoon Baking Powder

Choco lava cakes have to be served hot or warm. once they cool, the molten chocolate solidifies. But even at room temperature these lava cakes taste. Chocolate Lava Cakes are ultra right, decadent, and gourmet but surprisingly simple! Make them for Valentine's Day this year.

Instructions to make Choco Lava idli Cakes:
  1. At First, Grind the Bourbon Biscuits into a Fine Powder
  2. Add 1 Spoon of Baking Powder with the Powdered Biscuits
  3. Add Milk little by little until it becomes a Fine Batter
  4. Pour 1 Drop of Oil on all the parts of the idly Plate and Add the Batter only for Half the size of the Tray
  5. Then Add the Dark Chocolates into it
  6. After that, Close the Dark Chocolates by Adding the Remaining Batter to it
  7. Then Boil it for 15 Minutes and then we can take it out
  8. When we Cut those idlys, the Lava will come out Melting
  9. Our Choco Lava idly Cakes are Ready Now!!
