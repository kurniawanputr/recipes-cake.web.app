The ingredients needed to make Lemon Drizzle Cake:
  1. Get Cake mix
  2. Make ready butter
  3. Get caster sugar
  4. Take eggs
  5. Get self raising flour
  6. Prepare salt
  7. Take milk
  8. Get grated rind of 1 lemon
  9. Get Syrup
  10. Make ready juice of 1 lemon
  11. Get icing sugar

Instructions to make Lemon Drizzle Cake:
  1. Grease and line 2lb loaf tin
  2. Cream butter and sugar together
  3. Slowly add beaten egg
  4. Stir in flour and salt
  5. Mix in lemon rind and milk
  6. Pour into tin and bake for 35min at 180oC
  7. Heat icing sugar and lemon juice until it forms a syrup
  8. While cake is still warm and in tin, prick top and pour on the warm syrup
  9. Leave cake in tin until cold before taking out
