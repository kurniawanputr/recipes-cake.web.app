Instructions to make Banana-lemon cake:
  1. In a bowl sieve the flour, salt, cloves and ginger and set it aside
  2. In another bowl mash the ripe bananas and set aside
  3. In a separate bowl mix the eggs, cooking oil, vanilla essence, sugar and lemon rind
  4. Using a mixer or whisk mix them
  5. Add in the mashed bananas and beat
  6. Fold in the flour mixture alternating with lemon juice. Funally fold in the raisins
  7. Pour the batter in two well greased loaf tins and bake at 170°C for 45- 50 mins or until a skewers comes out clean when inserted in the cake
