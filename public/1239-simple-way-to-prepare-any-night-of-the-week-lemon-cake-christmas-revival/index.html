Steps to make Lemon Cake #Christmas Revival#:
  1. Pre heat oven @ 180 degrees, wash the lemons & grate them,set aside. Half them then squeeze their juice aka zest.
  2. In a bowl mix margarine & sugar till pale and fluffy. Crack the eggs and beat them thoroughly.
  3. Add the eggs in the bowl & mix well. Pour the lemon zest & mix well.
  4. Add sifted flour mixed with baking powder, fold in as you add the grated lemon.
  5. Grease the baking tin & dust it, pour in the batter, and let it bake for 40 - 45 minutes.The
  6. The cake baked evenly & the taste is divine, serve with tea.
