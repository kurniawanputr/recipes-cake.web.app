The ingredients needed to make Bologna Cake:
  1. Take 2 tblspoon. Onion powder
  2. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire
  3. Take 1 lb. bologna
  4. Get 1 pkg. Cream cheese; softened
  5. Take Green onions (optional)
  6. Get 3 olives sliced (Optional)
  7. Make ready Ritz Crackers
  8. Make ready Cheez Whiz
Instructions to make Bologna Cake:
  1. Mix onion, Worcestershire Sauce, Cream Cheese til smooth.
  2. Layer each Bologna with Cream Cheese, stacking on top of each other.
  3. Add remaining cream cheese all over, top and sides.
  4. Spread Cheez Whiz to your liking
  5. Top with olives and green onions (optional)
  6. Refrigerate and slice like a cake. Serve with Riz Crackers
