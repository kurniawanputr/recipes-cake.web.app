The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Coconut Cream Soup:
  1. Prepare Mushroom (chopped)
  2. Prepare Garlic (chopped) - 2 tb spoon
  3. Get Garlic (chopped) - 2 tb spoon
  4. Get coconut oil - 1 tbl spoon
  5. Prepare coconut flour(optional) -
  6. Get mixed bean (boiled and smashed) -
  7. Take coconut milk -
  8. Get water:
  9. Make ready black seeds - 1/2 tbl spoon
  10. Make ready Himalayan pink salt/ regular salt :
  11. Prepare cilantro (chopped) 1 tbl spoon
  12. Get ketchup, soy sauce, green chili water - for final serving
Instructions to make Mushroom Coconut Cream Soup:
  1. Add garlic to the hot pan, then add mushroom, stir for 2 minutes. Add salt, black seeds, coconut flour (you can add corn flour here, I did not want carb, so I avoided that)
  2. Stir and add water. Add smashed beans to thicken the soup. Finally add coconut milk and cilantro. Serve hot with sauce and chili water. Very tasty, very healthy, almost no carb soup.
  3. Enjoy with your family!
