Instructions to make Marble cake recipe:
  1. In a bowl mix sugar and margarine till creamy
  2. Add eggs one by one,add 1/4 tbsp of vanilla essence
  3. Sieve flour and baking powder and mix gently
  4. Pour half of the mixture in your greased tin and the other half mix with cocoa and then pour it on the tin
  5. Bake for 45 mins,ice your cake and decorate as you wish
