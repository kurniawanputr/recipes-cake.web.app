The ingredients needed to make Mug Cakes:
  1. Take 3 tbsp Wheat Flour
  2. Take 2 tbsp Powdered Sugar
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Baking Powder
  4. Make ready 1 pinch Baking Soda
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Vanilla Essence
  6. Take 5 tbsp Milk
  7. Take 2 tbsp Oil (Any refined oil)
Instructions to make Mug Cakes:
  1. Add all dry ingredients in Microwave safe Mug. And mix well.
  2. Add all wet ingredients and mix well.
  3. Microwave it for 2 mins. Your Mug Cake is ready. Enjoy!
