The ingredients needed to make Mango cup cakes:
  1. Prepare 1 cup All-purpose flour maida
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  3. Take 1/4 tsp Baking soda
  4. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  5. Take 1/2 cup Natural organic sugar
  6. Take 2 Mango
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Oil
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 cup Milk according to need for better

Instructions to make Mango cup cakes:
