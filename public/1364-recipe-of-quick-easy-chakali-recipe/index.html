Steps to make Easy chakali recipe:
  1. Grind coriander, cumin and ajwain seeds to a fine powder.
  2. Roast rice flour, besan, and udad flour together for 8 to 10 minutes on a medium flame. We will be using half of this mixture for making chakali.
  3. Mix half of this roasted flour with sabudana powder and poha powder. Measure 1 cup of this mixture to use for the recipe. The rest can be stored at room temperature for upto a month.
  4. Boil 1 cup of water. Add salt, chilly powder, turmeric powder, hing, coriander, cumin and carrom seeds powder, sesame seeds and oil to the boiling water.
  5. Turn off the stove and then add 1 cup of the flour mixture. Cover and let it stand for ten minutes.
  6. When it cools down knead it into a dough.
  7. Using a chakali maker, shape it into chakalis and fry in oil on a medium flame till it becomes crisp.
  8. Enjoy this savory snack on special festivals like diwali or enjoy it as a tea time snack anyday!
