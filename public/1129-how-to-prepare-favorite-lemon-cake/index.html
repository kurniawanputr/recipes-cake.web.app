The ingredients needed to make Lemon Cake:
  1. Take for the lemon cake:
  2. Prepare unsalted butter
  3. Make ready zest of 2 lemons
  4. Make ready icing sugar
  5. Prepare medium eggs
  6. Make ready freshly squeezed lemon juice
  7. Get plain flour
  8. Take salt
  9. Get baking powder
  10. Take for the syrup:
  11. Take lemon juice
  12. Get water
  13. Get icing sugar
  14. Get honey
Steps to make Lemon Cake:
  1. For the cake: melt butter in a microwave. Whisk in the sugar. Grate in the lemon zest. Add in the eggs one at a time. Mix in the lemon juice
  2. Sift in flour, salt and baking powder. Don't overmix
  3. Pour in a greased and lined baking pan. Bake at 160°C for about 55 min
  4. Meanwhile, make the syrup: combine sugar, lemon juice and water in a small saucepan. Let syrup boil for a minute on high heat. Take off heat and mix in the honey
  5. Pour the syrup over the warm cake. Let cool completely and enjoy :)
