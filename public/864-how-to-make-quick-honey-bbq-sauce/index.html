The ingredients needed to make Honey bbq sauce:
  1. Make ready 1 cup ketchup
  2. Take 1/2 cup brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoons honey
  5. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  7. Take 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon onion powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps to make Honey bbq sauce:
  1. Combine all ingredients for honey bbq sauce in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Bring sauce to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove sauce from heat and let cool
  4. Store in an airtight glass container for up to 2 weeks.
