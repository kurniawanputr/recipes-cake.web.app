The ingredients needed to make Old Fashioned Pound Cake:
  1. Make ready 2 cup Cake Flour
  2. Get 1/4 tsp Mace
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 cup shortening
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Get 5 eggs
  8. Take 2 tbsp milk
Steps to make Old Fashioned Pound Cake:
  1. Blend dry ingredients, sift 3 times.
  2. Put shortening and sugar in mixing bowl. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Add vanilla. Add eggs one at a time. NOTE: eggs should be at room temperature. Mix with a hand mixer on low speed.
  4. Add dry ingredients and milk - 3 additions of dry and 2 additions of milk. Start with flour and end with flour.
  5. Grease tube pan generously and then flour.
  6. Bake for 1 hour in preheated 350°F oven.
