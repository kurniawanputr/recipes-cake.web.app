The ingredients needed to make Plain Cup Cakes:
  1. Take Oil half cup
  2. Make ready 1.5 cup Flour Maida
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Corn flour
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp Baking soda
  5. Take Milk warm half cup Vinegar 1 tsp
  6. Prepare 1 cup powdered Sugar
  7. Take 2 Egg
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp Coco powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla essence
Instructions to make Plain Cup Cakes:
  1. Grind sugar into fine powder. - - Seive flour, cornflour, baking powder and baking soda thrice.
  2. In warm milk add vinegar mix and keep aside. (buttermilk is ready)
  3. Beat egg and sugar until it turns foamy, now add oil and mix until the colour of mixture become pale. Add essence.
  4. Gradually add flour mixture and milk mix with beater for few seconds. (Don't over beat). - Fold mixture with spatula.
  5. Take half cup mixture and mix coco powder in it to make pattern. (My daughter can't make it properly please ignore)
  6. Grease the cup cake liners and fill with plain cake mixture 1 /3. Add 1 tsp coco mixture and make pattern.
