The ingredients needed to make Instant healthy dark chocolate cake:
  1. Take 6 tbsp wheat flour
  2. Prepare 5 tbsp jaggery
  3. Get 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp chopped dark chocolates
  5. Prepare 0.5 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 0.25 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 7 tbsp milk
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
