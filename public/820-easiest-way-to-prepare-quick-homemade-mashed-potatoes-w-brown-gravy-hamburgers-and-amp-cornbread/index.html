Instructions to make Homemade mashed potatoes w/ brown gravy hamburgers & cornbread!:
  1. Take your ground beef put it in a pan with the bellpepper and onions and brown it
  2. Get your potatoes diced them in small squares or however you would like. I leave mine skin on put a pot of water on medium high heat add a tablespoon of salt let them boil
  3. Pour both boxes of cornbread mix into a bowl add the 2 eggs, a teaspoon of oil, and 1/2 cup of milk mix it all together and put in the oven on 375 degrees for 20 mins
  4. Once the potatoes are done boiling drain the water off of them and put them in a bowl with 1/2 stick of butter, 1 1/2 cup of milk, teaspoon of sour cream and mix them all together
  5. Add 1 1/2 cup of water to a pot pour the gravy packs in stir till it comes to a boil
  6. Take the patties you made out of the ground beef and lay them on your plate with the mashed potatoes and put the gravy on them
