The ingredients needed to make Lemon Cake Roll:
  1. Get LEMON CAKE ROLL
  2. Prepare large eggs
  3. Get granulated sugar
  4. Prepare fresh lemon juice
  5. Get the zest of one lemon
  6. Get salt
  7. Take baking powder
  8. Prepare all-purpose flour
  9. Make ready vanilla extract
  10. Take confectioner's sugar for dusting to roll cake
  11. Get LEMON WHIPPED CREAM FILLING
  12. Make ready recipe attached in direction step #8
  13. Prepare GARNISH
  14. Get confectioner's sugar mixed with 1/2 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  15. Get fresh strawberrys and blackberries
  16. Make ready whipped cream, optional
Steps to make Lemon Cake Roll:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 10 by 15 inch jelly roll pan with bakers spray. Line pan with parchment oaper and spray parchment paper with bakers spray
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl, set aside
  3. In a medium bowl beat eggs until light and frothy, 2 minutes, beat in sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest and vanilla
  4. Add flour mixture to egg mixture and mix just until blended
  5. Spread batter in prepared pan using an off set spatula to reach all corners
  6. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes, meanwhile sprinkle confectioner's sugar on a clean kitchen towel to turn the cake on to when done. As soon as cake is done turn over onto confectioner's dusted towel
  7. Carefully remove parchment paper and roll cake with towel and cool on a rack in town completely
  8. Unrolll cake and spread Lemon Whipped Cream Filling filling, recipe attached below evenly over cake, roll cake up enclosing filling - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/357016-lemon-whipped-cream
  9. Combine confectioner's sugar and cocoa in a small bowl and dust top of cake with it. Chill cake at least 2 hours to set before cutting
  10. Garnish ca ke with Strawberrys, blackberries and offer whipped cream to go with it
