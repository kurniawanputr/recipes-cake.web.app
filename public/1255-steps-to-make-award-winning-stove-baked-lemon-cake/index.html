The ingredients needed to make Stove baked lemon cake:
  1. Take 375 g flour
  2. Make ready 185 g butter
  3. Get 170 g sugar
  4. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 1/4 cup lemon juice
  6. Prepare 1/2 (1 cup) milk
  7. Take 3 eggs
  8. Take Alittle sultanas
