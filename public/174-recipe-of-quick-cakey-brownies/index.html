The ingredients needed to make Cakey Brownies:
  1. Make ready 125 g chocolate bar
  2. Make ready 60 g butter
  3. Take 250 g sugar
  4. Make ready 3 eggs
  5. Make ready 4 tsp cocoa powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 180 g flour
Steps to make Cakey Brownies:
  1. Preheated oven to 175 deg C
  2. Melt chocolate bar and butter using double boiler until well combined. Lift it out.
  3. Add sugar and stir well until chilled.
  4. Add the eggs, one by one.
  5. Add cocoa powder, salt and baking powder.
  6. Add flour and fold the batter until it combined perfectly.
  7. Pour it into a square baking tray and bake for 30 minutes.
  8. After 30 minutes, take out from the oven and let it cooled down before you cut it into pieces.
