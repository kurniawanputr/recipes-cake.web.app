Instructions to make Potato Bread roll:
  1. Boil 4 large potatoes
  2. Cook one packet of chicken with lemon pepper seasoning
  3. Cut some onion into small pieces
  4. Peel and cut a carrot
  5. Mix the boiled potatoes and put paprika,salt,pepper, with garlic powder till combined
  6. Mix in the chicken with the potatoes
  7. Cut the crust off of some bread and roll them out till flat
  8. Put the potato mix on the bread
  9. Now fry till golden brown
  10. Enjoy 🥰
