The ingredients needed to make Double Lemon Cake:
  1. Make ready Cake
  2. Get instant lemon pudding
  3. Get water
  4. Prepare eggs
  5. Prepare oil
  6. Prepare lemon juice
  7. Get yellow or lemon cake
  8. Get Syrup
  9. Take powdered sugar
  10. Prepare lemon juice
  11. Get melted butter
Instructions to make Double Lemon Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Beat cake ingredients for two minutes.
  3. Lightly grease a 9x13 inch pan.
  4. Pour in cake batter into pan and bake for 30-40 minutes.
  5. Cool cake for 10 minutes.
  6. Pierce with fork and pour syrup over cake.
