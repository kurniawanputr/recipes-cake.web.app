The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Heart Cakes:
  1. Take 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
  3. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking soda
  5. Get 4 tsp cocoa powder
  6. Get 1/2 cup Lukewarm milk
  7. Prepare 1 tsp vinegar
  8. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence
  9. Take 1/4 cup oil
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup condensed milk
  11. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp chocolate sauce
  13. Prepare 25 grams chocolate
  14. Make ready 2 tbsp Pistachio or pumpkin seeds for decoration
Steps to make Chocolate Heart Cakes:
  1. Grease a cake mould and keep it aside.
  2. Heat up 1 cup salt in a cooker or kadhai.
  3. Combine milk, vinegar and vanilla essence and mix well.
  4. Sift flour baking powder baking soda and cocoa powder.
  5. In a bowl add butter sugar and oil mix with a whisker.
  6. Then add condensed milk and mix well.
  7. Add all dry ingredients into the wet material to make cake batter.
  8. Pour the batter into cake mould bake into the preheated cooker for 40 minutes.
  9. Let the cake be cool, now cut it in the shape of hearts with the help of cookies cutter.
  10. Heat up the chocolate sauce for 2 minutes then add grated chocolate and mix well.
  11. Pour it on cakes and decorate with pistachios or seeds.
