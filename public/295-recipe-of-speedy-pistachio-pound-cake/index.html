The ingredients needed to make Pistachio Pound Cake:
  1. Take 1 box Duncan Hines cake mix
  2. Get 1 cup vegetable oil
  3. Take 1 cup sour cream
  4. Make ready 1 oz pistachio puddimg mix
  5. Get 4 eggs
  6. Prepare 1 cup chopped pecans
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar

Instructions to make Pistachio Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 325.
  2. Thoroughly mix cake mix, oil, sour cream, pudding, and eggs.
  3. Pour half the batter into a greased bundt pan.
  4. In a separate bowl mix pecans, cinnamon, and sugar (topping).
  5. Sprinkle half the topping over the batter.
  6. Pour remaining batter over topping.
  7. Sprinkle remaining topping on batter.
  8. Bake for 60 mins.
