Instructions to make Lemon cake:
  1. Line 3 9" tins and set the oven to 170° for 45mins
  2. Combine all the dry ingredients together and whisk
  3. Combine milk, eggs, lemon juice, lemon zest and oil and whisk together on medium speed for 1minute
  4. Sift in the dry ingredients and mix on low speed until well combined.
  5. Use a spatula to ensure all ingredients are well combined
  6. Divide mixture evenly into the three tins and bake @170° for 30-35 mins or until tester comes out clean
  7. Let the cakes cool for 10-15 mins before transferring to a cooling rack
  8. Decorate as desired and enjoy
