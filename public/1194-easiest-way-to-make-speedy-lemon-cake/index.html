Instructions to make Lemon Cake:
  1. In a bowl, put the butter and sugar and cream well til pale yellow.
  2. Add the eggs (one at a time) and beat til well combined.
  3. Add the lemon zest and mix well.
  4. Mix the dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
  5. Sift the flour mix in the creamed batter and fold gently.
  6. Add the yoghurt and mix
  7. Transfer the batter to the baking tray and bake at 180° for 45 mins.
  8. Allow the cake to cool on a rack.
  9. Serve with beverage of choice.
