Instructions to make Spiced sweetcorn potato cakes:
  1. Combine potato, spices, ginger, sweetcorn, finely diced onion, spinach and herbs in a mixing bowl.
  2. Beat the egg in a separate bowl.
  3. Use 1 tbsp of mixture and form a ball with your hands. Gently flatten.
  4. Dip into cornflour and egg. Place into pan with a little oil. Fry on low medium low heat for approximately 2 mins until golden then turn over.
  5. Serve warm with fresh sliced tomatoes and spinach leaves for a light meal.
