The ingredients needed to make Fish cakes:
  1. Get 8 big potatoes peeled and cooked
  2. Take 2 cups salmon or (any fish of your choice)
  3. Prepare 1/2 cups finely chopped parsely
  4. Get 1 cup bread crumbs
  5. Take 1 cup corn flour
  6. Prepare 1 table spoon black pepper
  7. Take 2 table spoon white pepper
  8. Take 2 spoon curry powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 tea spoon ginger
  10. Get to taste Salt and seasoning
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup finely chopped Habanero peppers
  12. Make ready 1/2 cups finely chopped Onions
  13. Make ready 3 big sized eggs
  14. Get Oil for frying

Steps to make Fish cakes:
  1. Allow the potatoes to cool down and then mashe them
  2. Pieces the fish in medium chunks
  3. Combine the mashed potatoes, fish, onions, parsley and habanero peppers together
  4. Add curry powder, salt and seasoning to taste and mix well
  5. Form a medium shaped paddy
  6. Mix white pepper, black pepper and salt to the corn flour
  7. Break and whisk the egg, add ginger powder, 1/2 spoon black pepper
  8. Deep the paddies in the corn flour
  9. Then dip in the eggs
  10. After dipping in the eggs coat them in breadcrumbs and shallow fry in oil
  11. Serve with ketchup or any dressing of your choice
  12. Add a sprinkle of parsley leaves for garnishing
  13. Can be taken with teaas breakfast or dinner
