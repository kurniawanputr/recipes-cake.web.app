The ingredients needed to make Orange cup cakes:
  1. Take 1 cup flour (maida)
  2. Get 3/4 cup sugur
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup vegetable oil/butter
  4. Get 1/4 cup orange juice
  5. Make ready 1 orange cut in slices/chunks
  6. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Take Pinch orange food colour
  8. Prepare 2 eggs

Steps to make Orange cup cakes:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C for 10-15 min.grease cupcake mould try with oil& dry maida.cut oranges in chunks
  2. Beat sugar and melted butter together.add 2 eggs in it one by one & beat together
  3. Add maida baking powder in egg mixture
  4. Add orange food colour in mixtrue
  5. Add orange juice in mixture
  6. Pour cupcake batter in grease baking mould tray.
  7. Add chunks of oranges in each mould.
  8. Keep tray in oven for 30-40 min.check in between as well. When cupcakes are firm from upside & leaves the corners of mould.turn off heat. Leave them to cool down a bit.then take out them.
  9. Serve delicious orange cupcakes with tea/coffee to elders or make them a part of your kids lunch box. Now i will enjoy with my kids.
