The ingredients needed to make Egg and Dairy Free Pound Cake:
  1. Prepare 100 grams Pancake mix
  2. Get 30 grams Oil
  3. Take 100 grams Unprocessed soy milk
  4. Get 30 grams Sugar
  5. Make ready 1 Banana
Instructions to make Egg and Dairy Free Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix together all the ingredients except for the banana.
  3. Set aside a couple of slices of the banana to use for decoration, and mash the rest. Mix it into the bowl from Step 2.
  4. Pour the mixture into a pound cake tin, and top it with thinly sliced pieces of banana for decoration.
  5. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes.
