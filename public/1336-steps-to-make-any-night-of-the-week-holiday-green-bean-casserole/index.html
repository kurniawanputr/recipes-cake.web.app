The ingredients needed to make Holiday Green Bean Casserole:
  1. Get cream of onion soup
  2. Prepare frozen cut green beans
  3. Get milk
  4. Prepare Generous splash Worcestershire sauce
  5. Prepare French fried onions
  6. Get pepper
  7. Get salt
Instructions to make Holiday Green Bean Casserole:
  1. Add the soup, milk, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper to a medium sized casserole dish. Mix well.
  2. Add the green beans and half the fried onions. Mix until smooth.
  3. Cover the dish with foil. Bake in a 350 degree oven for about 20 minutes or until bubbly around the edges.
  4. Cover the top with the remaining fried onions then bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, sans the foil. Let rest for a bit then enjoy!!
