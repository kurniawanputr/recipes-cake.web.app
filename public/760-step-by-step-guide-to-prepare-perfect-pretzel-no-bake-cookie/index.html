Instructions to make Pretzel no bake cookie:
  1. In a medium sauce pan melt the butter over medium heat. - Once melted, stir in milk, sugar, cocoa. - Bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat, stir in peanut buttter and vanilla until smooth. - Add in the old fashioned oats and pretzels. - Drop mixture by spoonfuls onto waxed paper and let cool completely.
  3. Cookies will set up and harden as they cool.
