The ingredients needed to make Vanilla pound cake:
  1. Make ready 150 gms flour
  2. Get 150 grams margarine
  3. Get 150 grams sugar
  4. Prepare Vanilla essence
  5. Take 3 eggs
  6. Take 2 tablespoon milk

Steps to make Vanilla pound cake:
  1. Measure all your dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add margarine
  3. Add eggs
  4. Add the essence and milk
  5. Whisk together till smooth
  6. Pour in your lined baking tin
  7. Bake for 40mins at 280 degrees in a preheated oven.
  8. Enjoy!
