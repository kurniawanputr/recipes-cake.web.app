The ingredients needed to make Easy Cranberry Salsa:
  1. Make ready can whole berry cranberry sauce
  2. Prepare fine chopped cilantro (or 1tbsp of dried)
  3. Take fine diced red onion (or sweet white)
  4. Prepare olive oil
  5. Get tspn kosher salt
  6. Prepare tspn cumin powder
  7. Prepare tspn fine mixed jalapeno pepper
  8. Prepare tspn coarse ground black pepper
  9. Take mild hot sauce
