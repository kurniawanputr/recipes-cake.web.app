Instructions to make Dora cakes:
  1. In a bowl seive All the dry ingredients and mix them thoroughly. Now add eggs one by one. Add vanilla essence, mix them well.
  2. Add milk as required to make it a pouring consistency batter.
  3. Heat a flat pan or tawa pour the batter with a ladle in the same quantity. Once done from one side it will come up by itself. Cook from both side.
  4. Make as many you want to make and keep them aside. When serving add Nutella or melted chocolate on the inner part of the dora covering it with the other dora of the same size. Enjoy!!
