The ingredients needed to make Eggless Zebra Cake (Eggless Cake Recipe):
  1. Prepare - Maida
  2. Make ready powdered sugar
  3. Take Vanilla essence -
  4. Prepare baking powder
  5. Take cocoa powder
  6. Take glass Milk
  7. Prepare required to make smooth batter
  8. Prepare oil
  9. Take baking soda
Steps to make Eggless Zebra Cake (Eggless Cake Recipe):
  1. Grease the baking utensil with oil and sprinkle Maida over it.
  2. Keep aside. Sieve Maida,baking powder, baking soda together for 2-3 times in a utensil.
  3. Now sieve powdered sugar into this mixture. Now add the oil than milk and form a smooth batter and also add vanilla essence. Divide the batter into 2 parts. To 1 part add cocoa powder and leave the other part as it is.
  4. Take the greased utensil and alternatively pour the batter with a spoon over one another. Make the design.
  5. Preheat the microwave atAt 180° and then bake at 180° for 26 mins on convection mode.
