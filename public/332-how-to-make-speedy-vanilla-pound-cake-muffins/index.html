The ingredients needed to make Vanilla pound cake muffins:
  1. Get 1/2 cup flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup curd
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp milk powder
  4. Get 1 tsp cornflour
  5. Take 100 gm butter
  6. Get 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  7. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essance
  8. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Get 1/8 tsp baking soda

Instructions to make Vanilla pound cake muffins:
  1. In a large mixing bowl add sugar and butter.
  2. Mix with help of electric beater. Add curd and mix well.
  3. Sieve flour,cornflour,milk powder, baking powder and baking soda.
  4. Mix all with cut and fold method and pour in muffin moulds.
  5. Bake in preheated oven at 180 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes.
  6. Lets transfer to cooling rack.
  7. Delicious muffins are ready to eat.
