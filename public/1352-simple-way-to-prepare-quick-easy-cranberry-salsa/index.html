The ingredients needed to make Easy Cranberry Salsa:
  1. Get can whole berry cranberry sauce
  2. Take fine chopped cilantro (or 1tbsp of dried)
  3. Take fine diced red onion (or sweet white)
  4. Make ready olive oil
  5. Make ready tspn kosher salt
  6. Prepare tspn cumin powder
  7. Make ready tspn fine mixed jalapeno pepper
  8. Make ready tspn coarse ground black pepper
  9. Prepare mild hot sauce
Instructions to make Easy Cranberry Salsa:
  1. Blend & refrig a few hours. (Flavors improve by 2nd day in refrig). Let stand awhile out of refrig & remix before serving.
  2. Blood orange or mandarin segments compliment serving this as a salsa.
