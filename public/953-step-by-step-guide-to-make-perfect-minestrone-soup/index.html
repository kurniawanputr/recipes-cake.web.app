The ingredients needed to make Minestrone Soup:
  1. Prepare each) diced tomatoes
  2. Make ready tomato paste
  3. Make ready sun-dried tomatoes
  4. Prepare vegetable stock (use a quality stock not broth)
  5. Take carrots, diced
  6. Take celery, diced
  7. Make ready white onion, diced
  8. Make ready garlic, minced
  9. Make ready dried oregano
  10. Get rosemary
  11. Take Italiano seasoning
  12. Prepare sea salt
  13. Make ready pepper
  14. Get each) red kidney beans, drained
  15. Take zucchini, diced
  16. Make ready Ditali pasta (tubular)
  17. Get frozen green beans, thawed
  18. Prepare baby spinach, chopped
Steps to make Minestrone Soup:
  1. Combine all ingredients accept the pasta in a large soup pot. Cover and simmer on low for 4 hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add pasta to the pot and simmer for 1 more hour. Serve
