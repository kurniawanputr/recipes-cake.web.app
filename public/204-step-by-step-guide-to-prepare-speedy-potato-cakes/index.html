The ingredients needed to make Potato Cakes:
  1. Get 3 slice Sandwich bread
  2. Get 1 Butter for spreading
  3. Make ready 1 cup Cold mash potato
  4. Make ready 1 cup Cheddar cheese, grated
  5. Make ready 1 Egg
  6. Take 1 tbsp Grated onion
  7. Get 2 Rasher bacon, chopped
  8. Get 1 Seasoning- garlic powder, sea salt & oregano.
  9. Prepare 1 A little smoked paprika for colour
Instructions to make Potato Cakes:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 190*C.
  2. Lightly butter each slice of bread . Cut into quarters and place buttered side down into a muffin tray.
  3. Mix mash potato with egg, cheese, onion, bacon and seasoning.
  4. Spoon the mixture into tray (on top of bread), sprinkle with paprika and bake for 20/30 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Get the kids to help you make them- they are a winner!
