The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cup cakes:
  1. Make ready 1 cup buttermilk
  2. Make ready 1 cup powdered sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup oil
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Take 1.25 cup all-purpose flour/wheat flour
  6. Take 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  7. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Take For Cupcake icing:
  10. Make ready 140 gms butter
  11. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  12. Take 2 tbsp milk
  13. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  14. Take 1/4 cup cocoa powder

Steps to make Chocolate cup cakes:
  1. Add buttermilk, oil, powdered sugar, vanilla extract in a bowl and mix these ingredients.
  2. Now seive flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and baking soda and add them in the mixture.
  3. Pour the batter in the cupcake moulds and bake it for 10-15mins at 180 degree.
  4. To make icing you need to take butter and beat it.
  5. Then add the sugar and beat it again and then add the vanilla extract and beat it again.
  6. Then add milk and cocoa powder and beat it for the last time.
  7. Pour mixture in piping bag and do the decoration on the cupcakes.
