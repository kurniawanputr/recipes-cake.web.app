The ingredients needed to make Brownie(Christian cakes):
  1. Get 200 gm Wheat flour
  2. Prepare 75 gm Sugar
  3. Get 100 gm Butter
  4. Get 1/2 cup Milk
  5. Make ready 50 gm Nuts crushed
  6. Make ready 1 pinch salt and baking powder
  7. Take 1 Eggs optional, if needed please add only
  8. Prepare 2 tsp Vanilla Essence
  9. Get Vegan
  10. Take Microwave Cooking
  11. Prepare European

Steps to make Brownie(Christian cakes):
  1. Blend all the ingredients in a beater.
  2. Set the batter in a greased baking dish.
  3. Preheat the microwave oven with convection mode.
  4. Set for baking at 180 degrees for 40 minutes.
  5. After baking, set the cake to cool and then take it out of the baking dish.
  6. Finally serve warm cut into squares.
  7. You could dress it with chocolate syrup before serving which is optional.
