The ingredients needed to make Chocolate pecan pie:
  1. Make ready Frozen pie crust (store bought)
  2. Prepare light corn syrup
  3. Take white sugar
  4. Prepare dark brown sugar
  5. Take butter
  6. Make ready flour
  7. Get unsweetened cocoa powder
  8. Take eggs
  9. Get pecans
  10. Prepare milk chocolate chips

Southerners often add a splash of bourbon or rum to the filling which. Cover bottom of pie crust with pecans. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs and melted butter. Add the corn syrup, sugar, bourbon and the chopped chocolate.

Instructions to make Chocolate pecan pie:
  1. Melt the butter and chocolate chips on the stove top on low heat
  2. Set oven to 350. Combine the flour, corn syrup and both sugars
  3. Stir in the unsweetened cocoa once the sugar mixture is combined.
  4. Pour the melted butter and chocolate chip combo into the mix
  5. Beat the eggs in the mix until smooth. It’ll be kind of like brownie batter
  6. Pour the filling into your pie crust and sprinkle chopped pecans on the top.
  7. Put into the oven on top rack for 30-45 minutes! NOTE: the middle will jiggle a little bit when it’s done, but not the sides. The center will firm more once its cooled. The texture should be thick, fudgy rich and almost like a brownie!
