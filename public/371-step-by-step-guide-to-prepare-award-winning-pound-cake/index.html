The ingredients needed to make Pound cake:
  1. Make ready 3 large eggs
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp milk room temp
  3. Take 1 1/2 tspn vanilla
  4. Take 1 1/2 cups flour preferably cake flour
  5. Prepare 1 tspn baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup + 1/3 cup butter
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar

Instructions to make Pound cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 177 degree C and place rack in centre of oven
  2. Butter 9 × 5 inch pan
  3. In a bowl whisk together eggs,milk and vanilla
  4. In a bowl of your electric mixer,place the dry ingred i.e flour,baking powder nd sugar and mix on low speed fr 30 sec
  5. Add butter nd half the egg mixture.mix on low speed until moistened
  6. Increase mixer to medium speed and beat fr abt a min. Scrape down sides of bowl
  7. Gradually add remaining egg mixture,in 2 additions,beating abt 30sec aftr each addition
  8. Pour the batter in pan and smooth the top wit spoon. Bake fr abt 55 to 65 mins or until toothpick inserted in the centre comes out clean
  9. Remov frm oven and let it cool fr 10mins
