The ingredients needed to make Pound Cake ☆ Using Leftover Whipped Cream:
  1. Take 120 grams Cake flour
  2. Prepare 60 grams Sugar
  3. Get 2 grams Baking powder
  4. Take 70 grams Whipped cream (heavy cream)
  5. Make ready 1 Egg
  6. Take 50 ml Milk
Steps to make Pound Cake ☆ Using Leftover Whipped Cream:
  1. Put the flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl and stir with a whisk.
  2. Preheat the oven to 170°C. Lightly grease the cake pan with oil.
  3. Add the cream, egg, and milk to the bowl from Step 1 and mix together. Add your fillings if you prefer.
  4. Pour the batter from Step 3 into the pan and bake in the oven at 170°C for 40 minutes.
