Steps to make Cauliflower cakes:
  1. Microwave the cauliflower for 8 minutes. Squeeze all liquids out. Mash, break into small pieces
  2. Shred the cheese. In a bowl mix all the ingredients except oil, together well. Form into small cakes. Chill them at least 30 minutes, to keep firm. I put mine in the freezer.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry till crispy. Move to paper towels to absorb excessive oils.
  4. Serve hope you enjoy!
