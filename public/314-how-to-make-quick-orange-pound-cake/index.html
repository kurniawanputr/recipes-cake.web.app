The ingredients needed to make Orange pound cake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup All purpose flour (maida)
  2. Make ready 50 gm Milk powder
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Baking powder
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  5. Make ready 1 cup Curd
  6. Take 1 cup water
  7. Make ready 1 cup milk
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp Orange juice
  9. Make ready 1 tsp orange zest
  10. Get 1 tsp Orange essence
  11. Prepare to taste Sugar
  12. Prepare as needed butter

Steps to make Orange pound cake:
  1. Mix dry ingredients and sieve well - Mix curd, milk and water - Beat butter and sugar - Add orange zest, juice and essence till well combined
  2. Add dry and wet ingredients alternately and fold. - - Line a 6inch pan and bake in a preheated oven at 170 degrees for 25-30mins. - - Cool completely and then unmould
  3. Enjoy with a cup of tea
