The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Pound Cake:
  1. Take 8 oz cream cheese
  2. Make ready 3 stick butter
  3. Take 1/2 cup Crisco
  4. Get 3 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 6 eggs
  6. Prepare 3 cup all purpose flour
  7. Make ready 1 tsp almond flavoring
Instructions to make Cream Cheese Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 275°
  2. Cream butter, cream cheese, and Crisco
  3. Add sugar and mix well
  4. Add eggs and flour alternately and mix well
  5. Add almond flavoring and mix well.
  6. Spray bundt pan with Baker's Joy spray
  7. Pour cake mixture into pan and spread evenly
  8. Bake 90-120 minutes or until golden brown
  9. Enjoy!
