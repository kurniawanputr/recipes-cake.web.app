Instructions to make Chocolate cup cakes:
  1. In a bowl sift together maida, cocoa powder,baking powder baking soda,salt and set aside
  2. In another bowl beat together oil and sugar until creamy in texture then add eggs and beat again for 7 minutes. Add buttermilk and chocolate essence and beat for again 1 minute.
  3. Now fold the all dry ingredients in the batter and mix with the spatula. Now arrange cupcake liner in tray and pour cup cake batter and bake for 20 to 25 minutes
  4. Heat the cream on double boiler then add crushed chocolate in cream and mix it well then add liquid glucose in and mix with hand whisk. now Cover the mixture and leave to set at room temperature for 3 hours
  5. Now pipe cupcake and enjoy it
