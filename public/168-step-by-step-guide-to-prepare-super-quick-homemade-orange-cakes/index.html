The ingredients needed to make Orange Cakes:
  1. Prepare 3 cups flour
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 2 teaspoon orange zest
  4. Get 2 teaspoon lemon zest
  5. Take 1/2 cup sweet orange juice
  6. Get 250 g butter
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  8. Take 5 large eggs
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon orange flavor
  10. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla flavor
  11. Prepare 1 tablespoon orange colour
Steps to make Orange Cakes:
  1. Cream your butter and sugar till it become fluffy.
  2. In another bowl put ur sieve flour, baking powder, orange zest, lemon zest together. (all dry ingredients)
  3. Whisk your eggs well till it foam
  4. In ur well cream butter and sugar, pour in ur eggs, lemon and lime juice, flavors and colour.
  5. Add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and cream well.
  6. Prepare ur oven and bake pan.
  7. Bake well and decorate
