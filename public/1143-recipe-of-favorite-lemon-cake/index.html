Steps to make Lemon cake:
  1. Pre heat the oven
  2. Cream in the butter and sugar until fluffy
  3. Add flour little by little as incorporating with the mix
  4. On a separate bowl beat the eggs
  5. Mix the eggs on the mixture add your lemon juice
  6. Grease the tin and pour in the mixture
  7. Take to oven and bake for about 40mins
  8. Serve as a dessert
