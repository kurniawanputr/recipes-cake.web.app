The ingredients needed to make Sugar Cakes:
  1. Prepare 2 cup sugar
  2. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  3. Prepare 3 eggs
  4. Take 1 cup veg. shortening
  5. Get 1 cup buttermilk
  6. Take 4 1/2 cup flour
  7. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  9. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Get 1 granulated sugar (for sprinkling)
Steps to make Sugar Cakes:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Beat sugar, salt, eggs and veg. shortening.
  3. Add buttermilk and vanilla extract.
  4. Sift together flower, baking soda, baking powder, slowly add to wet mix.
  5. Mix till combined
  6. Place rounded tablespoonful onto a greased or parchment lined baking sheet.
  7. Sprinkle with granulated sugar.
  8. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes till light brown.
  9. Remove to rack and let cool.
