The ingredients needed to make Mango and dates pound cake:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cups all-purpose Flour/ Maida
  2. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Take 1/2 cup mango puree
  5. Get 1/2 cup ghee or refined oil
  6. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  7. Take 1 egg
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped mangoes
  10. Take 1/2 cup chopped dates
  11. Get As needed Sliced dates
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup mango

Instructions to make Mango and dates pound cake:
  1. Sieve the maida, baking powder, salt, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl take the sugar and ghee or oil and whip it for 5 minutes. Then add the egg or yoghurt for eggless cake. If adding egg then whip the mixture till it becomes light yellow in colour. If adding yoghurt then no need to whip much.
  3. Add the mango puree and vanilla essence to the egg mixture or yoghurt mixture for eggless cake. Mix again. Then add the maida mixture and incorporate slowly to avoid overmixing.
  4. Add the chopped mangoes and dates. Then pour the mixture in a greased baking tin and put the sliced mangoes and dates on top.
  5. Bake the cake till a toothpick inserted comes out clean.check after 30 minutes. Serve with a cup of hot tea.
