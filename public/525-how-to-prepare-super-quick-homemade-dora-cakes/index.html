The ingredients needed to make Dora cakes:
  1. Get 2 Cups Maida (All-purpose flour)
  2. Prepare 2 Tablespoons powder sugar
  3. Take 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  4. Prepare 1 pinch baking soda
  5. Make ready 1/4 Teaspoon salt
  6. Make ready 1 Cup lukewarm Milk
  7. Take 4 teaspoon melted butter mix with 1/2 teaspoon of refined Oil
  8. Get Garnishing:
  9. Get as required Chocolate Sauce, butter or honey

Steps to make Dora cakes:
  1. Take a mixing bowl pour their Maida, baking powder, 2 teaspoon of melted butter, powder sugar and mix slowly…. slowly with Milk and stir it properly and keep it aside for 10 mins.
  2. Now Switch on the gas flame and place a frying pan add there Butter and refine oil mix just spread it properly and wipe it with a tissue paper remove all it's excess oil or butter.
  3. Now pour a ladle full of batter and wait for just 1 or 2 mins it will increase it's volume by it's own and just flip it to other side and wait for again 1 to 2 mins on slow and medium flame.
  4. Then repet the same process with the rest batter and enjoy.
  5. Garnish it with chocolate sauce, honey or butter as you wish.
