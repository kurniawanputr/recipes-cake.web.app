The ingredients needed to make Cup cakes:
  1. Make ready 1 cup flour
  2. Get 3 eggs
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  5. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  8. Get as required Sprinklers to decorate
  9. Take as required Frosting of your choice

Steps to make Cup cakes:
  1. Separate egg white and yolks. Add sugar and vanilla essence to white and beat till stiff peaks form.
  2. Add rest of the ingredients to flour and mix well.
  3. Gradually add egg white in fold and mix method.
  4. Preheat the oven for 350’f. Pour the cake batter in cake liner till 3/4 of it's size.
  5. Bake the cake for 15 minutes. Allow to cool and insert toothpick to check it’s done.
  6. Decorate with frosting and sprinklers.
