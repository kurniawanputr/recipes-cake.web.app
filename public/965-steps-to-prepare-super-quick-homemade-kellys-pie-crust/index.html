The ingredients needed to make Kelly’s Pie Crust:
  1. Take ☆4 cups flour
  2. Take ☆2 cups crisco
  3. Get ☆2 tsp salt
  4. Prepare ☆1 TBSP sugar
  5. Get ●1 TBSP vinegar
  6. Make ready ●1 egg
  7. Take ●1/2 cup water
Instructions to make Kelly’s Pie Crust:
  1. Mix all the ingredients of ☆ in a large bowl. Don’t mix it too well. Leave some crisco in chunks to have good texture.
  2. Mix all the ingredients of ● and pour into the flour mixture.
  3. Spread plastic wrap on a large cutting board and sprinkle flour on it.
  4. Put half of the flour mixture on the wrap and make a round shape. Do the same thing for the 2nd half of the flour mixture. Put both of them in a zip lock and rest them in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes to 3 days.
