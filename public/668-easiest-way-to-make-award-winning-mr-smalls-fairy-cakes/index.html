The ingredients needed to make Mr small’s fairy cakes:

Steps to make Mr small’s fairy cakes:
  1. Sieve the self raising flour and baking powder into a large bowl and add the butter and the sugar.
  2. Crack the eggs into a separate bowl and beat before adding to the large bowl.
  3. Mix all until it has turned into a mixture.
  4. Put the mixture evenly into cale cases. Make sure they are all similar sizes so they don’t burn.
  5. Take them out of the oven when they look done and add whichever toppings you like (I left them plain)
  6. Enjoy!!!
