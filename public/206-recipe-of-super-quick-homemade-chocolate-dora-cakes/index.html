The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Dora cakes:
  1. Get 1 cup Maida/All purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 cup milk
  4. Prepare 2 teaspoon honey
  5. Prepare Pinch baking soda
  6. Prepare 2 -3 drops vanilla essence
  7. Take 1 banana
  8. Make ready as needed Chocolate spread
Instructions to make Chocolate Dora cakes:
  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a mixture and set a side for 5 minutes then Take a pan and put 1 tablespoon of batter lower the flame cover it with a lid before that grease pan with some butter
  2. Now after one side of pancake is cooked flip the other side and cook well and after cooking take some chocolate spread and put the same in between two dora cakes now dorayaki is ready to eat
