Instructions to make Vegan pot pie - using chickpeas flour & whole wheat aata:
  1. Sun dry the chickpeas for 2 days and grind it to fine powder and seive it to collect the chickpeas flour
  2. To a mixing bowl add whole wheat atta, the chickpeas flour, salt, coconut oil(1 tbsp) & some water
  3. Knead the flour and make a soft firm dough & rest for 15 minutes
  4. To a pan add coconut oil(1tbsp), onions, carrots & mixed vegetables (carrot, cooked chickpeas, potato, green peas,cauliflower) & saute for few minutes
  5. Now add cashew paste & coconut milk and cook until the vegetables are cooked well. (Inner filling is ready)
  6. Now take a small portion from the prepared dough & flatten it with chapathi roller & make a circular shaped layer
  7. Grease the baking cup with Coconut oil & spread the prepared layer as the bottom crust
  8. Use some fork to make some marks on the bottom crust (check the attached picture)
  9. Now add some vegetable fillings (don't add too much, add half the volume of the baking cup you chosed)
  10. Now cover the filling with the top crust (you can make any pattern with the dough)
  11. Brush some almond milk & coconut oil over the top crust
  12. To a pressure cooker add some salt & place a stand. Preheat the cooker for 15 minutes
  13. Now place the prepared baking tray & bake for 30-45 minutes
  14. Cool down the pie & remove it from the vessel
