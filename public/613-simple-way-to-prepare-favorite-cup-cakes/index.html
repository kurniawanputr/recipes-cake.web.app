Steps to make Cup cakes:
  1. Crush oreo and parle g biscuit separately
  2. Add little little pinches of milk and make a smooth batter
  3. Now add baking powder 1/4 spoon each (1/4 in parle g and 1/4 in oroes)
  4. Mix well
  5. Now put a pan on a gas add one cup water put a stand in the pan
  6. Pour the 2 batters in a paper cups
  7. Now out the paper cups in a plate and put the plate into the pre heated pan
  8. Cover with a lid..use cloth also for better result
  9. At low flame for 10 minutes ready to serve
  10. Take off the lid and put a toothpick if toothpick comes clean from the cake
  11. Your cake is ready now decorate it with choco chips or Jam….kids like it very much💕
