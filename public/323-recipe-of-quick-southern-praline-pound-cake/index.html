The ingredients needed to make Southern Praline Pound Cake:
  1. Get cake
  2. Make ready 1 box (18.25oz.) Betty Crocker Butter Pecan cake mix
  3. Prepare 1 can coconut pecan frosting
  4. Make ready 4 eggs
  5. Get 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  6. Get 1 cup water
  7. Take 1 cup chopped pecans
  8. Make ready 1 9 or 10 inch bundt pan
  9. Take 1 powdered sugar
Steps to make Southern Praline Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 (175 degrees C). Spray a 9 or 10 inch Bundt pan with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Combine the cake mix with the frosting, eggs, oil, water and 1/2 of the pecan pieces. Mix until combined.
  3. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 of pecans in the prepared Bundt pan then pour in the cake batter.
  4. Bake at 350°F (175 degrees C) for 50 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Sift powdered sugar over cooled cake
