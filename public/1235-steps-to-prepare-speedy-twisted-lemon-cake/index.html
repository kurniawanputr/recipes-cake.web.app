Instructions to make Twisted lemon cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degree and prepare your baking tin
  2. Measure all ingredients mix the dry ingredients and sieve.
  3. Using a hand mixture mix sugar n margarine until pale and fluffy
  4. Add one egg at a time mix for 1 min before adding another one.
  5. Add lemon zest lemon juice and vanilla essence mix until well incorporated
  6. Add sieved flour n baking powder in 3 bit fold well.
  7. Transfer into your prepared greased tin and tap gentle on the floor to remove any excess air
  8. Bake for 40 mins or until an inserted skewer comes out clean
  9. Let it cool and decorate ad per your desire.
