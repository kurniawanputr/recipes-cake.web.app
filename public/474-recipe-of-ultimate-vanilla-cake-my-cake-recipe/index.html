The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Cake #my cake recipe:
  1. Get plain flour
  2. Get sugar
  3. Take egg
  4. Prepare cooking oil
  5. Get baking soda
  6. Get vanilla essence
  7. Prepare baking powder
  8. Prepare drinking chocolate
  9. Take milk
Steps to make Vanilla Cake #my cake recipe:
  1. Sieve flour in a bowl.add the baking powder and baking soda
  2. Break the egg in a separate bowl and in sugar whisk.add in the milk and mix together.
  3. Add in the cooking oil and keep mixing.slowly add in the milk mix to the flour until it forms a nice smooth batter.
  4. Add in the vanilla essence and keep mixing.
  5. Divide the batter in half and in one mix in the drinking chocolate
  6. Grease a tin with oil and add in the chocolate batter first and then the plain one
  7. Place a sufuria on a pressure cooker and place a wire inside. Allow to heat for 5 minutes on low heat.
  8. Place the cake batter tin on the wire and cover.
  9. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes
  10. Allow to cool serve.
