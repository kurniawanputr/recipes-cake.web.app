The ingredients needed to make Corn Cakes:
  1. Take white corn meal mix
  2. Prepare granulated sugar
  3. Get frozen whole kernel corn
  4. Take milk
  5. Prepare pickled jalapeno peppers
  6. Take eggs
  7. Take vegetable oil
Instructions to make Corn Cakes:
  1. Preheat griddle to 350
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, combine corn meal and sugar.
  3. Blend remaining 5 ingredients until chunky.
  4. Add blended ingredients to mixing bowl. Stir to combine.
  5. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto griddle. Cook 2-3 minutes per side, flipping once.
  6. Makes 6-8
