Instructions to make Chocobourn cup cakes:
  1. In a bowl mix all-purpose flour, bournvita, baking powder, baking soda and salt to prepare a dry mix.
  2. In another bowl mix sugar, oil and little milk to prepare a wet mix. Whisk nicely to avoid any lumps.
  3. Now keep on adding small portions of dry mix into wet mix and whisk properly to prepare the batter. Add milk as per need to get the proper consistency of batter.
  4. Add tutti fruity to the batter and mix well.
  5. Now grease paper cups with oil and preheat the pressure cooker by adding little water and a ring in it for about 10 minutes.
  6. Put some batter into cups, then add chocolate and again batter and tap it gently. Sprinkle some tutti fruity. Do not fill cups more than 3/4.
  7. Bake the cups into the pressure cooker for around 20-25 minutes. Remember to remove the whistle.
  8. With the help of a toothpick check the cakes after 25 minutes.
  9. Let it cool and then unmould the cup cakes gently.
  10. Garnish with tutti fruity, gems and chocolate. Enjoy these chocolicious choco-bourn cup cakes.
