The ingredients needed to make Yummy Bread Dessert:
  1. Get bread pieces
  2. Get Ghee/refined oil
  3. Prepare Sugar syrup
  4. Make ready Crushed almonds
Instructions to make Yummy Bread Dessert:
  1. Firstly, remove the bread corner. Cut the bread slices into any shape and keep aside for 8-10 hours without cover.
  2. If it is dry and hard, dessert will make crunchy and yummy
  3. Heat oil in a pan, when oil is hot, set the gas on medium flame. fry the bread pieces for about 1 minute, flip and fry to both side.keep on the paper towel
  4. Dip the fried bread pieces into the sugar syrup for about 1 minute, flip and again dip for 1 minute. sugar syrup should light warm.
  5. For sugar syrup, click on my given recipe link - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/13764209-gulab-jamun?invite_token=8EBkrBBh16meBfkAwnRh5YK7&amp;shared_at=1604604158
  6. Spread crushed almond on the top of the bread pieces and ready to eat
