Whole Wheat Banana Walnut Cake Recipe

To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can cook whole wheat banana walnut cake recipe | eggless banana walnut cake using 9 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Banana Walnut Cake Recipe | Eggless Banana Walnut Cake:
  1. Make ready Wheat flour
  2. Make ready Milk
  3. Take Sugar
  4. Take Banana small
  5. Prepare Butterscotch/vanilla essence
  6. Take Baking soda
  7. Get Baking powder
  8. Make ready Oil
  9. Prepare Walnuts
Steps to make Whole Wheat Banana Walnut Cake Recipe | Eggless Banana Walnut Cake:
  1. Sieve wheat flour, baking soda and baking powder for 4-5 times and keep it aside.
  2. Take 1/4 cup milk, sugar and banana in mixer. - Add oil and vanilla essence to it and blend to smooth paste.
  3. Transfer to a bowl. - Add flour to it and mix it without lumps. - Add 1/4 cup milk and mix it to smooth batter.
  4. Then add half of chopped walnuts to it. - Grease the baking tray and transfer the batter to the tray.
  5. Preheat the oven to 175°C for 10 mins and bake in the same temperature for 20-25 minutes till tooth pick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Cool them in wire rack. - Slice and enjoy banana walnut cake
