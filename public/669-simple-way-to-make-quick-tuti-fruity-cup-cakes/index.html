Instructions to make Tuti fruity cup cakes:
  1. Sieve maida, baking powder and baking soda.
  2. In a bowl add curd and oil. Mix it well.
  3. Add sugar powder and mix it well.
  4. Now add maida mixture slowly and gradually. Mix it and add little little milk while mixing. Add vanilla essence also. Mix the batter
  5. Add dusty tuti fruity in batter.
  6. Fill the greased moulds 1/2 with batter. Put some tuti fruity over it.
  7. Bake in hot oven for 15-20 minutes. Or you can also bake it on gas. Place a kadahi put wire stand in it pre heat it for 5-7 minutes then put mould in it or bake for 15-20 minute on medium flame
  8. Enjoy yummy tuti fruity cup cake with tea.
